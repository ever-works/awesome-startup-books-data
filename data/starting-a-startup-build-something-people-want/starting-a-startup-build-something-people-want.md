## Features
- **End-to-end startup roadmap**
  - Takes founders from idea through validation, MVP, first customers, and early scaling.

- **Frameworks and tools**
  - 50+ actionable frameworks
  - Step-by-step templates
  - Real-world case studies

- **Idea validation & customer discovery**
  - How to test a startup idea before major time or money investment
  - Customer interviews for understanding real problems and needs
  - MVP testing to validate assumptions
  - Market validation to confirm demand and willingness to pay

- **Building a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)**
  - Feature prioritization to decide what to build first
  - Creating a development roadmap
  - Setting up user feedback loops to iterate based on real usage

- **Acquiring first customers**
  - Practical strategies to get first paying customers without a large marketing budget
  - Sales frameworks tailored for early-stage startups
  - Growth tactics to move from zero traction to initial momentum
  - Customer retention practices to keep and learn from early users

- **Scaling and fundraising**
  - Fundraising strategy and how to approach investors
  - Team-building guidance for early-stage hiring and organization design
  - Operations scaling to support growth beyond the initial MVP stage

- **Learning and iteration mindset**
  - Emphasis on rapid learning, iteration, and execution over relying on ideas alone
  - Focus on reducing wasted time, money, and effort by validating assumptions early

- **Intended audience**
  - First-time founders
  - Repeat founders
  - Builders stuck between idea and execution
  - Funded teams facing early growth pressure
  - Aspiring founders finally ready to ship a real product

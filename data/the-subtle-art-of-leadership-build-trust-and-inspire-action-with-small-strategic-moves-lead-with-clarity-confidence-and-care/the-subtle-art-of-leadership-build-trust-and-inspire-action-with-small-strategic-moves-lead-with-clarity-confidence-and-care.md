## The Subtle Art of Leadership

**Full Title:** The Subtle Art of Leadership: Build Trust and Inspire Action with Small, Strategic Moves. Lead with Clarity, Confidence, and Care.  
**Author:** Amy Long  
**Category:** Leadership & Management

### Overview
A leadership book focused on practical, small, strategic actions leaders can use to build trust, influence, and effective execution. It is especially useful for startup founders and leaders of small, fast-moving teams who need to lead with clarity, empathy, and consistency while creating a culture that can scale.

### Features
- Focuses on “small, strategic moves” as the core lever of effective leadership.
- Emphasizes building **trust** as a foundation for leadership.
- Provides guidance for **inspiring action** and improving team execution.
- Aims to help leaders communicate and operate with **clarity** and **confidence**.
- Stresses **care** and empathy in day-to-day leadership decisions.
- Tailored for:
  - Startup founders
  - Leaders of small, fast-moving teams
  - Anyone working to build a scalable, healthy company culture
- Oriented toward creating a **culture that scales** with the organization’s growth.
- Practical, action-driven approach rather than abstract leadership theory.
- Paperback format suitable for everyday reference and note-taking.

# Competitive Advantage: The Value Chain and Your P&L—Applying Michael Porter’s Value Chain Framework to Your Business

A chapter that introduces Michael Porter’s value chain framework and explains how to apply it to a company’s activities and P&L—helpful for startup founders seeking to understand and design competitive advantage into their business model and operations.

## **Disciplined Entrepreneurship: 24 Steps to a Successful Startup**

### **Overview**
**Author:** Bill Aulet  
**First Published:** January 1, 2013  
**Format:** Kindle Edition

### **Description**
"Disciplined Entrepreneurship" by Bill Aulet is a comprehensive guide aimed at helping aspiring entrepreneurs launch successful startups. The book breaks down the complex process of starting a business into 24 actionable steps, focusing on a disciplined approach to entrepreneurship.

### **Features**
- **Step-by-Step Guide:** The book offers a detailed, step-by-step methodology that simplifies the startup process.
- **24 Steps Framework:** Each step is clearly defined and explained, ensuring readers can follow the process sequentially.
- **Practical Advice:** Includes practical insights and tools that can be applied directly to startup projects.
- **Focus on Discipline:** Emphasizes the importance of discipline in the entrepreneurial journey, providing strategies to maintain focus and direction.
- **Applicable to Various Industries:** The principles outlined can be adapted to a wide range of industries and startup types.

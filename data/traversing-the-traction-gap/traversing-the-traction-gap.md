### Description

"Traversing the Traction Gap" is a strategic guide designed to help startups navigate the challenging phases of growth. Authored by Bruce Cleveland, the book offers a framework that emphasizes the importance of a market-first mindset to ensure successful go-to-market strategies.

- **Strategic Framework:** Provides a robust framework for startups to navigate growth phases effectively.

- **Growth Stages:** Guides startups through critical growth stages with practical insights.
- **Go-to-market Success:** Focuses on engineering successful market entry strategies.

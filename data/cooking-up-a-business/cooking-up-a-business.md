## Overview
“Cooking Up a Business” is a practical guide for aspiring entrepreneurs, centered on real food-startup founders. It focuses on how to validate a product idea, build a brand, navigate distribution, and scale a consumer packaged goods (CPG) or food-related business from the ground up.

## Features
- **Real founder case studies** from food and CPG startups, illustrating what worked and what didn’t.  
- **Idea validation guidance** including testing concepts, getting early feedback, and refining products before a full launch.  
- **Branding fundamentals** such as positioning, storytelling, packaging, and standing out on crowded shelves.  
- **Distribution strategies** covering how to move from local to broader markets, approach retailers, and think about channels.  
- **Scaling insights** on going from small batches to larger production, managing growth, and maintaining product quality.  
- **Consumer-product focus** tailored specifically to food and packaged-goods entrepreneurs, rather than generic startup advice.  
- **Practical, action-oriented tips** aimed at first-time founders building a business from scratch.

# The Innovator's Dilemma: When New Technologies Cause Great Firms to Fail

Influential innovation and strategy book by Clayton M. Christensen explaining how disruptive technologies can cause even well-managed incumbents to fail. Essential reading for startup founders to understand disruption theory, why startups can outcompete large firms, and how to identify and exploit disruptive opportunities in new markets.

## Overview

Patrick Lencioni argues that organizational health—not strategy, finance, or marketing—is the ultimate competitive advantage.

## The Four Disciplines

### 1. Build a Cohesive Leadership Team
- Trust among executives
- Healthy conflict
- Commitment to decisions
- Peer accountability
- Focus on collective results

### 2. Create Clarity
- Why do we exist?
- How do we behave?
- What do we do?
- How will we succeed?
- What is most important, right now?
- Who must do what?

### 3. Over-Communicate Clarity
- Repeat key messages 7x
- Multiple channels
- Cascading communication
- Reinforce constantly

### 4. Reinforce Clarity
- Hiring
- Performance management
- Rewards and recognition
- Dismissals

## Organizational Health Indicators

- Minimal politics
- Minimal confusion
- High morale
- High productivity
- Low turnover

## For Founders

- Health > smart
- Clarity prevents chaos
- Leadership team sets the tone
- Over-communication is impossible

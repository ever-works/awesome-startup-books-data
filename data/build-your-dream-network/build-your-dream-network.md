## Build Your Dream Network

**Author:** J. Kelly Hoey  
**Category:** Leadership & Management  
**Tags:** Networking, Strategies

### Overview
"Build Your Dream Network" is a comprehensive guide aimed at entrepreneurs and ambitious professionals who wish to harness the power of networking to achieve their business objectives. Written by networking strategist J. Kelly Hoey, this book emphasizes the significance of building and maintaining a supportive network.

### Features
- **Networking Strategies:** Offers actionable strategies to cultivate and expand networks that align with professional goals.
- **Relationship Building:** Insights into forging meaningful connections that contribute to personal and business growth.
- **Social Capital Design:** Encourages readers to intentionally design their relationships for career momentum.
- **Practical Advice:** Provides exercises and tips to transform networking concepts into tangible actions.

### Author's Expertise
Kelly Hoey is a renowned networking strategist and speaker, known for her contributions to major publications such as the New York Times, Inc., and Forbes. She has been recognized by leading platforms like Forbes and Fast Company for her innovative approach to networking.

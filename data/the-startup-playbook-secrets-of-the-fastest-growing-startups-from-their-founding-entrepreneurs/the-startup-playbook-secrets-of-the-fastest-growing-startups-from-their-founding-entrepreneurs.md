# The Startup Playbook: Secrets of the Fastest-Growing Startups from Their Founding Entrepreneurs

A practical guide for startup founders that distills lessons, strategies, and candid advice from the founding entrepreneurs of some of the fastest-growing startups. It covers early-stage validation, product-market fit, fundraising, hiring, and scaling, making it a highly relevant must-read for founders building a startup from scratch.

### Founder Fallout

**Type:** Book / Practical Workbook  
**Author:** Jeremy G. Barker  
**Category:** Biographies / Startup & Entrepreneurship  
**URL:** <https://www.amazon.com/Founder-Fallout-question-Before-equity-ebook/dp/B0FF2NX5SC>

---

#### Overview
Founder Fallout is a practical, no-nonsense workbook for startup founders and aspiring partners to work through before sharing equity or formalizing a co-founder relationship. It centers on tough, structured questions designed to uncover misalignment, clarify expectations, and prevent future partnership and equity conflicts.

---

#### Features
- **Question-Based Workbook Format**  
  - Contains 100+ (presented as 101 in the description) practical, “must-ask” questions.  
  - Designed to be worked through directly with current or potential co-founders.

- **Focus on Equity & Partnership Alignment**  
  - Helps founders address equity-splitting decisions before formal agreements.  
  - Guides conversations about ownership, contributions, and long-term expectations.

- **Pre-Agreement Risk Assessment**  
  - Aims to surface cracks in the relationship before they become serious issues.  
  - Encourages decisions to either commit confidently or walk away early.

- **Covers Common Founder Landmines**  
  - Partner misalignment and conflicting visions.  
  - Burnout and workload imbalance.  
  - Ego clashes and power struggles.  
  - Equity disputes and unfair perceived contribution.  
  - Emotional exits and personal relationship breakdowns.

- **Operational & Legal Considerations**  
  - Prompts on what to document and sign before launch.  
  - Encourages founders to clarify legal agreements and formal structures.  
  - Addresses role definition, decision-making authority, and responsibilities.

- **Conflict & Crisis Handling**  
  - Guides discussions on conflict-resolution mechanisms between founders.  
  - Considers how to operate and maintain respect during crises.  
  - Encourages planning for worst-case scenarios and exits.

- **Outcome-Oriented Framework**  
  - Structured to push teams toward a clear decision: commit (“hell yes”) or disengage (“walk away now”).  
  - Helps prevent late-stage resentment and costly buyouts.

- **Author Background**  
  - Written by Jeremy G. Barker, a serial entrepreneur and 8-figure founder.  
  - Draws on multiple company builds, rebuilds, and failures rather than academic theory.

- **Intended Use Cases**  
  - Startup founders considering taking on co-founders or partners.  
  - Investors, advisors, and accelerators performing partnership due diligence.  
  - Experienced entrepreneurs who previously had failed or painful partnerships.  
  - Individuals starting businesses with friends, spouses, siblings, or mentors.

---

#### Audience

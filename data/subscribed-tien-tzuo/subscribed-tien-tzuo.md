## Overview

Subscribed shows how the subscription model is transforming every industry and how companies can adapt to thrive.

## The Subscription Economy

Key principles:
- Customer-centricity over product-centricity
- Recurring revenue over one-time sales
- Usage-based pricing
- Continuous value delivery

## Making the Transition

1. Think customer relationships, not transactions
2. Embrace dynamic pricing
3. Invest in subscriber experiences
4. Build for growth, not just acquisition

## Success Stories

- Adobe's shift to Creative Cloud

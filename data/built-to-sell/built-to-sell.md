## Summary
"Built to Sell" by John Warrillow is a practical guide for entrepreneurs aiming to create businesses that can operate independently of their founders. The book presents a fictional narrative of a small business owner, Alex, who learns how to transform his advertising agency into a sellable enterprise. Through guidance from his mentor Ted, Alex discovers key strategies to make his business attractive to potential buyers.

## Features
- **Teachable:** Emphasizes the importance of developing products and services that can be easily taught and replicated by employees, reducing dependency on the owner.
- **Valuable:** Encourages entrepreneurs to specialize and excel in a niche area, avoiding price wars and enhancing business value.
- **Repeatable:** Focuses on creating recurring revenue streams by designing products that customers will need to purchase repeatedly.

### Hooked: How to Build Habit-Forming Products

**Author:** [Nir Eyal](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/7284116.Nir_Eyal)

**Category:** Customer Experience

**Tags:** User Engagement, Habit-Forming, Product Design

**Description:**
*Hooked* by Nir Eyal is a comprehensive guide that delves into the mechanics of creating habit-forming products. Eyal introduces the "Hook Model," a four-step approach that many successful companies use to foster customer engagement and retention without relying heavily on advertising.

**Features:**
- **The Hook Model:** A four-step framework that helps in embedding habitual behavior in users.
- **Real-World Examples:** Insights drawn from popular products like the iPhone, Twitter, Pinterest, and the Bible App.
- **Actionable Advice:** Step-by-step guidance for product managers, designers, marketers, and startup founders looking to enhance user engagement.
- **Research-Backed Strategies:** Based on years of research and practical application in product design and consumer psychology.
- **Target Audience:** Ideal for those interested in understanding the intersection of psychology, technology, and business.

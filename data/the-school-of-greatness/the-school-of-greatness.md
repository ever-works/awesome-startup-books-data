## The School of Greatness by Lewis Howes

![The School of Greatness](https://images.gr-assets.com/books/1439923723l/23848518.jpg)

### Description

**The School of Greatness** is a guide by Lewis Howes that lays out a comprehensive roadmap for achieving personal and professional excellence. It is an inspiring read for entrepreneurs and individuals striving to make a significant impact and leave a lasting legacy.

### Features

- **Focus on Personal Growth**: Encourages readers to pursue personal development and self-improvement.
- **Professional Excellence**: Offers insights and strategies for achieving success in one's career.
- **Inspiring Content**: Filled with motivational stories and actionable advice.
- **Legacy Building**: Guides readers on how to create a meaningful and enduring legacy.

### Additional Information

- **Category**: Personal Development
- **Tags**: Growth, Motivation, Legacy
- **Format**: Hardcover, 272 pages
- **First Published**: October 27, 2015

### Author

**Lewis Howes** is an accomplished author and motivational speaker known for empowering individuals to unlock their potential and achieve greatness in all aspects of life.

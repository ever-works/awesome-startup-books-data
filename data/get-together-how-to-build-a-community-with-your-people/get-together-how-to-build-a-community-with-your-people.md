## Overview

"Get Together" is a guidebook for building communities by Bailey Richardson, Kevin Huynh, and Kai Elmer Sotto of People & Company. Published by Stripe Press, the book reveals the secret to getting people together: build your community with people, not for them.

## Authors

In 2015, Bailey Richardson, Kevin Huynh, and Kai Elmer Sotto started People & Company, a strategy partner for organizations like Nike, Porsche, Substack, and Surfrider. In March 2021, People & Company was acquired by Substack. Bailey Richardson was an early Instagram hire who helped shape communities around Instagram, IDEO, and Pop-Up Magazine.

## Core Philosophy

The book's throughline is the simple concept: "build with," which lives in each recommendation through three stages of building a community:

- **Sparking the flame** - Starting your community
- **Stoking the fire** - Growing and engaging members  
- **Passing the torch** - Sustaining community leadership

## Key Features

- Practical frameworks for both online and offline community building
- Real-world case studies from successful communities
- Step-by-step guidance for community organizers
- Strategies for maintaining authentic engagement
- Tools for transitioning from founder-led to community-led

## Why It's Essential for Founders

Startup founders need to build communities around their products, brands, and missions. This book provides the playbook for creating loyal, engaged communities that drive word-of-mouth growth and product adoption. The authors draw from extensive experience helping major companies build communities, making their insights immediately applicable to startups at any stage.

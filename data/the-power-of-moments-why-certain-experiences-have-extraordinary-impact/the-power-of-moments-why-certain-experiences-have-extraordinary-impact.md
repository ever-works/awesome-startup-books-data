## Overview

Why do we remember certain experiences forever while others fade? The Heath brothers reveal the four elements that create defining moments—and how to engineer them deliberately.

## The Four Elements

**EPIC moments have:**
- **Elevation**: Rises above the everyday
- **Pride**: Captures us at our best
- **Insight**: Rewires our understanding
- **Connection**: Shared meaning with others

## Applications for Startups

- Onboarding: Make day one unforgettable
- Customer milestones: Celebrate their wins
- Company rituals: Create shared traditions
- Product experiences: Design aha moments

## Key Insights

- Don't just satisfy—create peaks
- Endings matter more than averages
- Recognize and celebrate achievements
- Break the script to create surprise

- Popsicle hotline at hotel
- Trial of the apprentice blacksmith
- First day at work transformations

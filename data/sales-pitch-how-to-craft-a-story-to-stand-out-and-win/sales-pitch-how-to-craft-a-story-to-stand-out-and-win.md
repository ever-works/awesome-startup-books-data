## Overview

Released on October 5, 2023, Sales Pitch builds on the concepts from the bestselling book Obviously Awesome. World-renowned product positioning and marketing expert April Dunford shows how to achieve success by building a narrative that clearly communicates how your product is different and better than anything else on the market.

## The Eight-Step Sales Pitch Structure

The book guides readers through a structured approach to creating sales pitches that resonate with potential customers and drive conversions. The framework helps balance technical details with compelling storytelling.

## Key Concepts

**Differentiated Value**: Dunford describes value as the stepping stone that customers need to get from features to determine why features matter. This concept sits at the core of product positioning and a good sales pitch.

## Practical Tools

April Dunford offers a combined 12-page workbook for both Obviously Awesome and Sales Pitch, including:
- The Positioning Canvas
- The Sales Pitch Storyboard

## About the Author

April Dunford is a globally recognized expert in positioning and market strategy with 25 years of experience as VP of marketing at a series of successful high-growth startups. She has launched 16 products into market.

## Target Audience

Ideal for entrepreneurs, salespeople, marketers, and business leaders in technology and B2B companies who need to communicate their product's value effectively in competitive markets.

## Overview

Oliver Burkeman challenges productivity culture: you have ~4,000 weeks to live. You won't get it all done. Choose what matters.

## Core Insight

The average human lifespan is about 4,000 weeks. Efficiency isn't the answer—acceptance and choice are.

## Key Principles

- You can't do everything
- Productivity hacks are avoidance
- FOMO is guaranteed
- Limits are liberating
- Do what only you can do

## For Founders

- Say no to almost everything
- Accept you'll miss opportunities
- Focus on what matters most
- Stop optimizing, start choosing
- Presence > productivity

## The Productivity Trap

More efficiency just creates more to do. The to-do list never ends. Make peace with incompletion.

## Wisdom

- Pay yourself first (with time)
- Embrace your finitude
- Choose meaningful constraints
- Joy comes from doing, not finishing

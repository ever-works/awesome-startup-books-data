# What Customers Want

*Written by Anthony W. Ulwick*

## Description
"What Customers Want" by Anthony W. Ulwick challenges the traditional customer-driven model by introducing the concept of "outcome-driven innovation." This approach offers a scientific methodology for understanding customer needs and developing products that deliver specific outcomes. It is particularly valuable for startups and businesses aiming to innovate effectively by aligning their product development with customer expectations.

## Features
- **Outcome-Driven Innovation:** A systematic approach to identifying and addressing customer needs.
- **Scientific Methodology:** Utilizes a structured process for innovation that goes beyond traditional customer feedback.
- **Focus on Outcomes:** Emphasizes creating products that achieve specific customer outcomes, rather than just meeting needs.
- **Practical Insights:** Offers actionable insights and strategies for startups to drive innovation and product development.

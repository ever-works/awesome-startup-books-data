## Startup CEO

**Name:** Startup CEO  
**Category:** Leadership & Management  
**Tags:** CEO, Leadership, Management  

### Description
*Startup CEO* by Matt Blumberg is a comprehensive guide aimed at first-time founder CEOs. It covers a wide range of topics essential for leading a startup, from hiring and firing to managing board meetings. The book serves as a valuable resource for understanding the dual roles of a CEO as both a manager and a leader, emphasizing the importance of excelling in both areas for a startup's success.

### Features
- **Comprehensive Coverage:** Detailed insights into various aspects of being a startup CEO, including team management, board relationships, and strategic planning.
- **Leadership Guidance:** Tips on how to develop and maintain a strong company culture and effectively motivate teams.
- **Practical Advice:** Offers real-world examples and practical advice to help first-time CEOs navigate the challenges of leading a startup.
- **Management Cycle:** Discusses a five-stage management cycle crucial for startup growth and sustainability.

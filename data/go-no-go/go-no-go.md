## Go. No Go.

### Overview
**Author:** Lauri Harrison  
**Published:** November 20, 2018  
**Format:** Kindle Edition  
**Pages:** 111  

**Description:**  
*Go. No Go.* is a practical guide designed for entrepreneurs who are in the initial stages of launching their startup ideas. Written by Lauri Harrison, this book provides a comprehensive and structured methodology for evaluating the viability and potential success of business concepts before they are launched.

### Features
- **Structured Approach:** Offers a step-by-step process for assessing business ideas.
- **Practical Guidance:** Provides practical advice and tools to help entrepreneurs make informed decisions.
- **Focus on Viability:** Emphasizes the importance of evaluating the feasibility of startup ideas before investing resources.

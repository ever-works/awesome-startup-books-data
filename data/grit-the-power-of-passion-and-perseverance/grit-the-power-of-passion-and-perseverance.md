## Overview

In this instant New York Times bestseller, pioneering psychologist Angela Duckworth shows anyone striving to succeed—be it parents, students, educators, athletes, or business people—that the secret to outstanding achievement is not talent but a special blend of passion and persistence she calls "grit."

## The Grit Formula

**Grit = Passion + Perseverance**

- Passion is not just enthusiasm; it's consistency over time
- Perseverance is about resilience and staying power
- Together, they predict success more accurately than IQ or talent

## Key Concepts

- **Effort Counts Twice**: Talent × Effort = Skill, and Skill × Effort = Achievement
- **The Four Pillars of Grit**: Interest, Practice, Purpose, Hope
- **Growth Mindset**: The belief that abilities can be developed
- **Deliberate Practice**: How to improve skills systematically
- **The Hard Thing Rule**: A family approach to building grit in children

## What Founders Will Learn

- Why passion and perseverance matter more than innate talent
- How to develop grit in yourself and your team
- The science behind sustained effort and achievement
- How to maintain motivation during difficult times
- Building a culture of grit in your organization
- The importance of purpose beyond just making money

## Research-Based Insights

Duckworth's research shows that:
- West Point cadets who scored highest on grit were most likely to complete training
- Teachers with high grit had better student outcomes
- Grit predicted success in the National Spelling Bee better than IQ

## Relevance for Startups

The startup journey requires sustained passion and perseverance through:
- Pivots and setbacks
- Funding challenges
- Product development struggles
- Team building difficulties
- Market rejection

Grit provides the framework for enduring these challenges successfully.

## About the Author

Angela Duckworth is a psychologist and popular TED speaker. She is a professor at the University of Pennsylvania and a MacArthur Fellow. Her research on grit has been featured in major media outlets worldwide.

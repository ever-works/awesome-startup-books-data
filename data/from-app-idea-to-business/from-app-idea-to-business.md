# From App Idea to Business

Step-by-step product management processes for startups transforming app ideas into viable businesses, including MVP development, validation, and launch strategies. Tailored use cases for mobile and software founders covering market fit and monetization. More practical for tech products vs. general PM frameworks like Inspired.

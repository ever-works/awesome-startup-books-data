## Overview

Reid Hoffman (LinkedIn) proposes replacing lifetime employment and free agency with alliance—tours of duty that benefit both company and employee.

## The Alliance Framework

### Tours of Duty
Time-bound commitments with clear missions:
- **Rotational**: Learn the company (6-12 months)
- **Transformational**: Achieve specific goal (2-4 years)
- **Foundational**: Core leadership (longer term)

### Network Intelligence
Employees extend company network:
- LinkedIn connections
- Industry insights
- Talent referrals
- Market intelligence

### Alumni Network
Former employees as lifetime assets:
- Boomerang hires
- Customer referrals
- Investors and advisors
- Brand ambassadors

## Key Principles

- Mutual investment, mutual benefit
- Honest career conversations
- Support employee growth (even if they leave)
- Alumni are assets, not traitors

## For Founders

- Stop pretending employees stay forever
- Design meaningful missions
- Invest in alumni relationships
- Be transparent about tours

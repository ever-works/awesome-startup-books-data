## Swipe to Unlock: The Primer on Technology and Business Strategy

### Overview
**Swipe to Unlock** by [Parth Detroja](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/17170106.Parth_Detroja), along with co-authors Neel Mehta and Aditya Agashe, is a comprehensive guide to understanding the intersection of technology and business strategy. This book is particularly beneficial for startup founders and entrepreneurs aiming to navigate the tech industry and leverage its dynamics for business success.

- **Authors**: Parth Detroja, Neel Mehta, Aditya Agashe
- **Publication Date**: September 1, 2017
- **Format**: Ebook
- **Pages**: 325
- **Category**: Business Strategy

### Description
**Swipe to Unlock** delves into the essential concepts of technology and business, providing readers with a clear understanding of how these two fields intersect and influence each other. It covers a broad spectrum of topics, including:

- The fundamentals of technology and its impact on modern business strategies.
- Insightful case studies that highlight real-world applications and strategies.
- Tools and frameworks for analyzing and approaching business problems in the tech industry.

### Features
- **Comprehensive Overview**: A thorough exploration of the current tech landscape and its implications for businesses.
- **Case Studies**: Real-world examples and scenarios that illustrate strategic business decisions in technology.
- **Strategic Insights**: Frameworks and tools to help readers develop strategic thinking and problem-solving skills.
- **Innovation Focus**: Emphasis on innovation and its role in business growth and sustainability.

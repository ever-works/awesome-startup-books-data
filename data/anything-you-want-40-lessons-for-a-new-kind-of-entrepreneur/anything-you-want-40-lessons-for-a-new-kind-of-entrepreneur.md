# Anything You Want: 40 Lessons for a New Kind of Entrepreneur

A concise, story-driven book by Derek Sivers that distills 40 lessons from building and selling his company CD Baby. It focuses on unconventional yet highly practical guidance for founders, emphasizing simplicity, customer obsession, and designing a business to serve your life—not the other way around. Widely recommended as a must-read for startup founders who want real-world, no‑nonsense insights rather than abstract theory.

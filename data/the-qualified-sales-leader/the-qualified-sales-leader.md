## MEDDPICC Framework

- **Metrics**: Quantifiable impact
- **Economic Buyer**: Who signs?
- **Decision Criteria**: How they'll choose
- **Decision Process**: Steps to close
- **Paper Process**: Legal/procurement
- **Identify Pain**: Business problem
- **Champion**: Internal advocate
- **Competition**: Who else are they considering?

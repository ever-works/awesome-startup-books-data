## Overview

Cindy Alvarez shows how to integrate customer development into product development. Learn to validate ideas through conversations, not surveys.

## Key Practices

- Interview early and often
- Ask about past behavior, not future intent
- Watch what they do, not what they say
- Look for patterns across interviews
- Build MVPs based on learning

## For Product Teams

- Who to talk to and when
- Questions that reveal truth
- Turning insights into features
- Continuous customer contact

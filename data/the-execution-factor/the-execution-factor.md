## The Execution Factor

**Author:** Kim Perell  
**Format:** Hardcover  
**Pages:** 288  
**Publication Date:** September 10, 2018  
**Category:** Productivity & Efficiency

### Description
"The Execution Factor" by Kim Perell is a crucial read for aspiring entrepreneurs and business professionals. The book delves into the importance of execution as a skill in the world of entrepreneurship. Perell offers readers practical strategies to transform their ideas into actionable plans, ultimately leading to business success.

### Features
- **Focus on Execution:** The book emphasizes execution as the key skill that drives successful entrepreneurship.
- **Practical Strategies:** Provides actionable steps and strategies to help entrepreneurs and business professionals achieve their goals.
- **Resource for Entrepreneurs:** Serves as a valuable guide for those looking to turn their business ideas into reality.

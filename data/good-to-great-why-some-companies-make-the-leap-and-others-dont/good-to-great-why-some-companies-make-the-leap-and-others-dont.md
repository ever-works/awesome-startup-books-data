# Good to Great: Why Some Companies Make the Leap and Others Don’t

Jim Collins’s management classic that analyzes how good companies become great and sustain performance over time. It introduces concepts like Level 5 leadership, hedgehog concept, and disciplined culture—frameworks that help startup founders think about building enduring, high-performing organizations beyond the early hustle phase.

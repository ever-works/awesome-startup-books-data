## Overview

Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion is a psychology book examining the key ways people can be influenced by compliance professionals. The book has sold over 3 million copies and has been translated into thirty languages.

## The Six Principles of Influence

### 1. Reciprocity
People feel obligated to give back to others who have given to them.

### 2. Commitment and Consistency
People like to be consistent with things they have previously said or done.

### 3. Social Proof
People will do things they see other people doing.

### 4. Authority
People tend to obey authority figures.

### 5. Liking
People are easily persuaded by people they like.

### 6. Scarcity
Perceived scarcity generates demand.

## Overview

The Great CEO Within teaches how to efficiently scale your business from startup to corporation by implementing a system of accountability, effective problem-solving, and transparent feedback. The book provides practical, tactical advice rather than abstract theory.

## About Matt Mochary

Matt Mochary coaches the CEOs of many of the fastest-scaling technology companies in Silicon Valley, including Coinbase, Plaid, Reddit, Brex, OpenAI, and Flexport. He is Silicon Valley's most sought-after CEO coach and created the Mochary Method in the early 2010s.

## The Mochary Method

The Mochary Method blends tactical, no-nonsense advice with human connection and mindfulness, resulting in a cheat code for leaders on how to run their companies effectively and efficiently.

## Book Structure

The book is split into the following parts:

### Individual Habits
Personal productivity systems and habits that make executives more effective.

### Team Habits  
Systems for team communication, meetings, and collaboration.

### Infrastructure
The organizational structures and processes needed to scale.

### Collaboration and Processes
How to build effective cross-functional collaboration.

## Key Features

- Practical templates and frameworks that can be implemented immediately
- Real examples from Silicon Valley's fastest-growing companies
- Focus on accountability systems
- Emphasis on transparent feedback
- Tools for effective delegation

## Origin

The book was originally passed "hand to hand" as a Google Doc for a few years before being published as an ebook and paperback, demonstrating its practical value to the startup community.

## Target Audience

CEOs, founders, and senior executives looking for tactical guidance on scaling their organizations, not theoretical frameworks.

# Crossing the Chasm, 3rd Edition: The Updated Version of the Insightful Guide on Bringing Cutting-Edge Products to the Mainstream

Geoffrey A. Moore’s classic on startup growth and technology adoption, explaining how innovative products can move from early adopters to the mainstream market. It introduces the technology adoption lifecycle and the concept of the “chasm,” offering concrete strategies for positioning, marketing, and scaling high-tech startups.

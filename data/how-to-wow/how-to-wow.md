# How to Wow

**Category:** Marketing & Sales  
**Tags:** Persuasion, Communication, Pitching

## Overview
“How to Wow: Proven Strategies for Selling Your Brilliant Self in Any Situation” by Frances Cole Jones is a practical communication and persuasion guide for founders and leaders. It focuses on how to present yourself and your ideas effectively in pitches, meetings, and media appearances—skills that are especially important when raising capital and selling a vision.

## Features
- **Founder-focused communication skills** – Tailored to people who need to represent a company or idea, such as startup founders and leaders.
- **Persuasion strategies** – Covers approaches for influencing others’ perception of you and your ideas in professional settings.
- **Pitching and fundraising support** – Helps readers structure and deliver compelling pitches when seeking investment or stakeholder buy-in.
- **Meeting effectiveness** – Provides guidance on presenting clearly and confidently in internal and external meetings.
- **Media appearance preparation** – Addresses how to show up effectively in interviews, panels, and other public-facing formats.
- **Soft-skill development** – Emphasizes persuasion, presence, and clarity as core soft skills for leadership and sales.

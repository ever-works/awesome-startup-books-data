# Obviously Awesome

## Overview
Obviously Awesome by April Dunford teaches founders how to effectively position their product in the market. It offers a practical, step-by-step process to ensure customers instantly grasp the product's value and relevance.

## Features
- Step-by-step positioning process
- Techniques to identify best-fit customers
- Methods to define and communicate unique value
- Exercises and real-world examples
- Guidance for new product launches and pivots

## Pricing
The book is available in various formats including paperback, hardcover, Kindle, and audiobook. Prices typically range from $15 to $30.

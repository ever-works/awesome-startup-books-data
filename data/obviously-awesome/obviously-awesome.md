## Overview

**Obviously Awesome** by April Dunford is a comprehensive guide on product positioning, specifically designed for startups and entrepreneurs. The book provides a structured approach to creating a compelling positioning statement, ensuring that your product resonates with its target audience.

## Features

- **Step-by-step Guide**: The book offers a detailed, methodical approach to positioning your product effectively in the market.
- **Real-world Examples**: It includes practical examples that illustrate successful positioning strategies.
- **Understanding Competition**: Learn to identify your productâ€™s unique value and how it stands against competitors.
- **Target Audience Insight**: Provides insights on defining and understanding your ideal customer base.
- **Dynamic Positioning**: Emphasizes the importance of adaptability in positioning strategies to reflect market and product changes.

## Key Concepts

1. **Positioning Importance**: Highlights the critical role of positioning in ensuring a product's visibility and appeal in a crowded market.
2. **Contextual Framing**: Teaches the art of framing a product to highlight its unique attributes and value.
3. **Five Critical Components**:
   - Problem Identification: Understanding the issues your product solves.
   - Differentiation: Pinpointing and leveraging unique product features.
   - Customer Definition: Identifying the ideal customer profile.
   - Market Context: Choosing the right market environment for your product.
   - Flexibility: Adapting positioning strategies as the market and product evolve.

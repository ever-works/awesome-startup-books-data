## Overview

Gino Wickman presents the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS)—a holistic approach to getting what you want from your business through vision, traction, and health.

## The Six Key Components

### 1. Vision
- Get everyone 100% on the same page
- Answer 8 questions:
  - Core Values?
  - Core Focus?
  - 10-Year Target?
  - Marketing Strategy?
  - 3-Year Picture?
  - 1-Year Plan?
  - Quarterly Rocks?
  - Issues?

### 2. People
- Right people in right seats
- GWC: Get it, Want it, Capacity to do it
- Accountability Chart (not org chart)
- Core Values alignment

### 3. Data
- 5-15 key numbers
- Weekly scorecard
- Leading indicators
- Everyone has a number

### 4. Issues
- Identify, Discuss, Solve (IDS)
- Issues list
- Prioritize and solve systematically
- Don't let issues linger

### 5. Process
- Document core processes
- Followed by All (FBA)
- 3-7 core processes
- 20% documented gives 80% value

### 6. Traction
- Rocks: 90-day priorities
- Meeting Pulse: Weekly L10 meetings
- Everyone has 3-7 Rocks per quarter
- Hit 80%+ of Rocks

## The Weekly L10 Meeting

90 minutes, same agenda:
- 5 min: Segue (good news)
- 5 min: Scorecard review
- 5 min: Rock review
- 15 min: Customer/employee headlines
- 60 min: IDS (solve top 3 issues)
- 5 min: Conclude (recap, cascade, rating)

## Rocks System

- 3-7 priorities per quarter
- Must be SMART goals
- 80%+ completion rate
- Company → Department → Individual

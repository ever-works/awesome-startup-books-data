# The Richest Man in Babylon - The Original 1926 Classic (Reader's Library Classics)

Personal finance and wealth-building classic by George S. Clason, offering timeless principles on saving, investing, and managing money through parables set in ancient Babylon. While not startup-specific, many founders consider it a foundational mindset book for financial discipline and long-term wealth creation, making it a valuable complementary read for entrepreneurs.

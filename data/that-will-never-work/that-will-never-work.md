## That Will Never Work

**Author:** Marc Randolph  
**Pages:** 336, Hardcover  
**First Published:** September 17, 2019  
**Category:** Disruption & Innovation  
**Tags:** Innovation, Perseverance, Problem-solving

### Overview
"That Will Never Work" is an insightful journey into the inception and evolution of Netflix, as narrated by its co-founder, Marc Randolph. The book delves into the challenges, failures, and triumphs that paved the way for Netflix's groundbreaking success. It is an inspiring read for anyone interested in entrepreneurship, especially those intrigued by the dynamics of innovation and perseverance.

### Features
- **Insider's Perspective:** Offers a unique behind-the-scenes look at the early days of Netflix.
- **Innovation and Problem-Solving:** Highlights the creative problem-solving approaches that were instrumental in overcoming initial setbacks.
- **Lessons in Perseverance:** Shares personal anecdotes and the relentless pursuit of turning a nascent idea into a global phenomenon.

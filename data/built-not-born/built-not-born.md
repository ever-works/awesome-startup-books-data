# Built Not Born

![Built Not Born Book Cover](https://images-na.ssl-images-amazon.com/images/I/81vpsIs58WL.jpg)

**Author:** Tom Golisano  
**Publisher:** HarperCollins Leadership  

---

### Overview
"Built Not Born" is an insightful autobiography where Tom Golisano, founder of Paychex, narrates his entrepreneurial odyssey. This book serves as a guide for budding entrepreneurs, detailing how to build and maintain a successful business from scratch while navigating the inherent risks and challenges.

---

### Features
- **Entrepreneurial Insights:** Golisano shares his firsthand experiences and strategies on how to transform a small startup into a billion-dollar enterprise.
- **Risk Navigation:** Learn how to manage and embrace the risks of entrepreneurship.
- **Unconventional Strategies:** Discover why questioning conventional wisdom can unlock unique business opportunities.
- **Effective Negotiations:** Understand the power of the "pregnant pause" in negotiations and interviews.
- **Legal Advice:** Gain knowledge on the importance of prenuptial and postnuptial agreements for business owners.
- **Business Plan Presentation:** Insights into what potential buyers and investors seek in business plans.
- **Growth & Leadership Strategies:** Learn the key strategies that drove Paychex's growth and sustained profitability.

## Overview

The Dip is a short, powerful book about knowing when to stick and when to quit. Seth Godin shows that winners quit fast, quit often, and quit without guilt—until they commit to beating the Dip.

## The Dip

Every new project starts out exciting and fun. Then it gets harder and less fun, until it hits a low point—the Dip. The Dip is the long slog between starting and mastery.

## Key Concepts

**The Dip**: The long stretch between beginner's luck and real accomplishment

**The Cul-de-Sac**: A dead end where effort doesn't lead to progress

**The Cliff**: When quitting becomes painful and expensive

## Strategic Quitting

- Winners quit the right stuff at the right time
- Quitting before you start is better than quitting in the middle
- Focus your resources on opportunities where you can be the best

## Applications for Startups

- Deciding which products to build
- Choosing markets to enter
- Determining when to pivot
- Allocating limited resources

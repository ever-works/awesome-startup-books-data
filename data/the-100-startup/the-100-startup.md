## The $100 Startup

**Author:** Chris Guillebeau

**Category:** Startup Methodologies

**Tags:** Bootstrapping, Entrepreneurship, Minimal Investment

**Website:** [100startup.com](https://100startup.com/)

---

### Description

*The $100 Startup* is a book by Chris Guillebeau that serves as a guide for aspiring entrepreneurs who wish to start and run a successful business with minimal investment. The book emphasizes creating income from a modest starting point, often $100 or less, and focuses on finding the intersection between personal passion and market demand.

---

### Features

- **Case Studies:** The book is built around 50 intriguing case studies of individuals who have successfully turned small investments into profitable businesses.
- **Actionable Insights:** It provides specific financial details, startup actions, and common pitfalls faced by entrepreneurs.
- **Entrepreneurial Philosophy:** Emphasizes the importance of action over extensive planning and the power of leveraging existing skills and passions.
- **Practical Advice:** Offers step-by-step guidance on how to start small, test ideas, and scale.

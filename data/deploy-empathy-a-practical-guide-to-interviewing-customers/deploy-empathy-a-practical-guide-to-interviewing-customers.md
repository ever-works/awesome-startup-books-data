## Overview

Deploy Empathy teaches the skill of talking to your customers - learning to truly listen to them - so that you can pull out their hidden needs, desires, and processes. Written by Michele Hansen, CEO & Co-Founder of Geocodio, a SaaS company bootstrapped to multi-millions in annual revenue, the book draws on thousands of customer interviews across B2C and B2B contexts.

## Key Features

- Sample scripts and templates for customer interviews
- Frameworks for analyzing customer feedback
- Guidance on using AI in customer research
- Customer interview workshop guide
- Multiple analysis methods with worked examples
- Practical approaches for different business stages

## What You'll Learn

- How to structure effective customer interviews
- Techniques for asking questions that reveal true customer needs
- Methods for analyzing and acting on customer insights
- Ways to integrate customer research into product development
- How to interview customers at different stages (pre-launch, growth, churn prevention)

## Relationship to Other Books

Described as "a fantastic addition to the customer interviewing line-up of resources - a logical next step after reading The Mom Test." While The Mom Test is more geared towards the pre-launch/discovery phase, Deploy Empathy covers other phases as well such as stopping churn, getting more customers, and testing.

## Overview

Indistractable: How to Control Your Attention and Choose Your Life by Nir Eyal reveals the Achilles' heel of distraction and provides a guidebook for getting the best of technology without letting it get the best of us. Published in 2019, it has become essential reading for anyone struggling with focus in the digital age.

## About Nir Eyal

Nir Eyal writes, consults, and teaches about the intersection of psychology, technology, and human potential. He previously taught at the Stanford Graduate School of Business and the Hasso Plattner Institute of Design at Stanford. He is the author of the international bestsellers "Hooked: How to Build Habit-Forming Products" and "Indistractable," which have sold over 1 million copies in more than 30 languages.

## Core Insight

Eyal argues that external triggers (notifications, emails, interruptions) aren't the root cause of distraction. The true distraction triggers are internal: fear of loneliness, aversion to boredom, anxiety, and discomfort. Time management is pain management.

## The Indistractable Model

The book presents a comprehensive four-part model:

### 1. Master Internal Triggers
- Identify psychological triggers that lead to distraction
- Learn to manage discomfort without seeking escape
- Understand the role of internal states in driving behavior
- Develop healthier responses to negative emotions

### 2. Make Time for Traction
- Use timeboxing to take control of your schedule
- Turn your values into time in your calendar
- Schedule time for yourself, relationships, and work
- Prevent distraction by planning ahead

### 3. Hack Back External Triggers
- Minimize interruptions from technology
- Redesign your environment for focus
- Manage workplace distractions
- Control smartphone and computer notifications

### 4. Prevent Distraction with Pacts
- Create precommitments that prevent distraction
- Use effort pacts, price pacts, and identity pacts
- Build systems that make distraction harder

## Key Strategies

- **Reimagine Internal Triggers** - Look for discomfort that precedes distraction and write down triggers
- **Reimagine the Task** - Find the fun in what you're doing
- **Reimagine Your Temperament** - You have the power to call yourself indistractable
- **10-Minute Rule** - When tempted by distraction, wait 10 minutes before giving in

## Why It's Essential for Founders

Founders face constant distractions—emails, Slack messages, investor requests, customer complaints, team questions. The ability to maintain focus on high-leverage activities determines startup success. Indistractable provides a battle-tested system for protecting attention in an interruption-driven world.

## Application to Startup Life

Founders can apply these principles to:

- Maintain deep work sessions for strategic thinking
- Build company cultures that respect focus time
- Model healthy technology habits for their teams
- Prevent burnout by managing internal triggers
- Make deliberate choices about where to invest attention

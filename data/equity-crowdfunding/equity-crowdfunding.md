## Equity Crowdfunding: A Guide for Startups

**Author:** [Nathan Rose](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/7707546.Nathan_Rose)  
**Category:** Fundraising & Investment  
**Tags:** Crowdfunding, Capital, Investment  
**Published:** October 31, 2016

### Overview
"Equity Crowdfunding" by Nathan Rose is a comprehensive guide designed to help startups and growing companies navigate the complex world of equity crowdfunding. This book provides insights and strategies essential for raising capital by offering shares to the public, thus combining investment with marketing exposure.

### Features
- **Understanding Fintech:** Learn about the fintech advancements that have made equity crowdfunding a viable option.
- **Funding Comparisons:** Analyze the differences between equity crowdfunding and other funding methods like bootstrapping, family & friends, incubators, venture capital, and Kickstarter.
- **Common Pitfalls:** Identify and avoid common mistakes that can hinder your crowdfunding campaign.
- **Momentum Building:** Discover techniques to build momentum, such as a case study where a company raised Â£1 million in just 96 seconds.
- **Marketing Strategies:** Develop a marketing plan that creates anticipation and drives investment.
- **Campaign Recovery:** Learn how to revive a stalled campaign and regain investor interest.
- **Real-World Insights:** Gain knowledge from 20 companies and 12 leading platforms that have successfully utilized equity crowdfunding.

### Practical Insights
- **Step-by-Step Guidance:** A detailed guide on selecting platforms, crafting pitches, and attracting investors.
- **Global Perspectives:** Contributions from countries including the UK, USA, France, Germany, and more, offering a diverse range of experiences and strategies.

### Target Audience
This book is an invaluable resource for startups, incubators, accelerators, co-working spaces, universities, education institutions, and business networks seeking to explore or leverage equity crowdfunding.

## Overview

"Lucky or Smart?" by Bo Peabody, a serial entrepreneur, explores the interplay of luck and skill in entrepreneurial success, offering insights into navigating the challenges of starting and growing a business.

## Key Insights from Recommender

- Described as "insight into some of the unique trials entrepreneurs face." – Mark Peter Davis, managing partner at Interplay Ventures
- Shows how to "put themselves in the right place at the right time and set the conditions for success."

## Relevance to Startups

The book provides practical advice and reflections from an experienced entrepreneur, helping founders understand the role of preparation, timing, and adaptability in achieving success.

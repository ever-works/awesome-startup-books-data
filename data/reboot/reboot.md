## Reboot

### Description
**Reboot** is a thought-provoking book by Jerry Colonna that delves into the intersection of leadership and personal growth. Through a combination of leadership insights and personal anecdotes, Colonna explores how childhood experiences influence leadership abilities, offering a distinctive perspective tailored for startup founders.

### Features
- **Leadership Insights**: Provides a fresh lens on leadership, focusing on personal development and self-awareness.
- **Personal Stories**: Includes engaging stories that demonstrate the real-world application of leadership principles.
- **Focus on Childhood Experiences**: Examines the impact of early life experiences on leadership style and effectiveness.
- **Target Audience**: Geared towards startup founders and those interested in personal growth and leadership.

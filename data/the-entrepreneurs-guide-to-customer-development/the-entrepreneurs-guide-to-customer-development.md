### Description
"The Entrepreneur's Guide to Customer Development" offers a practical approach to understanding the essential process of customer development, a critical component for startups striving to achieve product-market fit. Authored by Brant Cooper, this guide serves as an accessible cheat sheet to "The Four Steps to the Epiphany," breaking down complex concepts into actionable insights for entrepreneurs.

### Features
- **Practical Guidance:** Offers step-by-step instructions on implementing customer development strategies.
- **Focus on Product-Market Fit:** Emphasizes the importance of aligning your product with market needs.
- **Understanding Customer Needs:** Provides tools and techniques to uncover what customers truly want.
- **Startup-Friendly:** Written in a concise and clear manner, making it ideal for time-strapped entrepreneurs.

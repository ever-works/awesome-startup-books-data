## Overview

David Gage shows how to create partnership charters that address issues before they become problems. Based on decades mediating partnership disputes.

## The Partnership Charter

Address upfront:
- Decision-making authority
- Financial contributions
- Time commitments
- Exit scenarios
- Dispute resolution
- Values and vision

## Key Topics

- Equity splits and vesting
- Roles and responsibilities
- What if someone wants out?
- Handling disagreements
- Buy-sell agreements

## Why It Matters

Most partnerships fail due to unaddressed expectations. A charter prevents 90% of future conflicts.

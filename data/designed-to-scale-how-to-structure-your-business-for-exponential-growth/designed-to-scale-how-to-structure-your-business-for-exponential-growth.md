## Designed to Scale: How to Structure Your Business for Exponential Growth

**Type:** Business / Startup Scaling Book  
**Author:** Lex Sisney  
**Format:** Book (organizational design & scaling frameworks)

### Overview
“Designed to Scale” is a practical guide for founders and leaders on how to architect a company for sustainable, exponential growth. It focuses on how to design the structure of your business—teams, roles, and operating systems—so the organization can handle each new stage of scale without breaking its culture or internal processes.

### Features
- **Scaling-focused frameworks** for moving from early traction to growth while keeping the organization stable and aligned.
- **Organizational design guidance** on how to structure teams and reporting lines for high-growth environments.
- **Role design and clarity** to ensure responsibilities, ownership, and decision rights are well defined as the company grows.
- **Team architecture** principles for assembling and evolving teams that can support larger, more complex operations.
- **Operational alignment** strategies so processes, tools, and communication rhythms scale smoothly with headcount and revenue.
- **Stage-of-growth planning** to anticipate structural changes required at different phases of company growth.
- **Culture-preserving structure** approaches to prevent rapid scaling from undermining company values and ways of working.
- **Founder and leadership focus** with examples and guidance tailored to startup leaders transitioning from scrappy execution to structured growth.

### Use Cases
- Startup founders moving from product–market fit to rapid scaling.
- Leaders tasked with reorganizing teams for the next stage of growth.
- Operators and HR/People leaders designing roles and org charts for scale.

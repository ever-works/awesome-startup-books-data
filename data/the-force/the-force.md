# The Force

**Category:** Leadership & Management  
**Brand:** Penguin Random House  
**Format:** Book  
**Slug:** `the-force`

A business and leadership-oriented book (not related to Star Wars) that examines power, influence, and organizational dynamics. It is aimed at founders and leaders who want to better understand how authority, culture, and motivation operate inside growing companies.

![The Force Cover](https://images.penguinrandomhouse.com/cover/9780345376251)

---

## Features

- **Focus on power and influence**  
  Explores how power is created, maintained, and used within organizations, and how leaders can use influence ethically and effectively.

- **Organizational dynamics**  
  Looks at how structures, roles, and informal networks shape decision-making and performance in a company.

- **Authority and leadership styles**  
  Discusses different approaches to authority, their impact on teams, and how leaders can adapt their style as a company grows.

- **Company culture**  
  Examines how culture forms, how it can be intentionally shaped, and how it influences behavior, performance, and retention.

- **Motivation and engagement**  
  Addresses what drives people inside organizations, including intrinsic and extrinsic motivators, and how leaders can foster long-term engagement.

- **Relevance for founders and growing companies**  
  Oriented toward leaders who are scaling organizations and need insight into how internal dynamics change as the company grows.

- **Applicable to business and leadership contexts**  
  Though written as a general leadership book, the concepts are applicable to startups, small businesses, and larger organizations alike.

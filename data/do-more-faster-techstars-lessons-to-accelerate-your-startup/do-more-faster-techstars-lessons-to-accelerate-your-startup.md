# Do More Faster: Techstars Lessons to Accelerate Your Startup

Brad Feld compiles accelerator tactics for founders to accelerate growth through rapid iteration, team building, and customer validation. Key metrics include weekly progress velocity and milestone achievement for scaling early teams. Vs. Blitzscaling, it focuses on structured acceleration for pre-scale startups.

# Subscribed

Subscribed: Why the Subscription Model Will Be Your Company’s Future - and What to Do About It by Tien Tzuo is a business book that explains how to build and grow subscription-based companies, covering recurring revenue, customer lifetime value, and business model transformation—highly relevant for startup founders designing scalable SaaS and subscription startups.

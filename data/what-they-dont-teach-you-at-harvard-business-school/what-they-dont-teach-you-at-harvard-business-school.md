## Description

“What They Don't Teach You at Harvard Business School: Notes from a Street‑Smart Executive” is a practical business book focused on the real‑world side of doing business that often isn’t covered in formal education. Drawing on the author’s experience, it offers actionable lessons in sales, negotiation, and managing professional relationships—particularly valuable for entrepreneurs, startup founders, and managers who need pragmatic, experience‑based guidance rather than theory.

- **Real‑world, experience‑based insights**
  - Focuses on what the author calls “street‑smart” business skills learned in practice rather than in the classroom.
  - Emphasizes how business is actually conducted in day‑to‑day situations.

  - Covers practical techniques and attitudes for selling products, services, and ideas.

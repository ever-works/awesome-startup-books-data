## Overview

The Art of the Start 2.0 is Guy Kawasaki's completely revised and expanded edition of his classic guide to launching any endeavor.

## Core Topics

- The Art of Starting
- The Art of Positioning
- The Art of Pitching
- The Art of Writing a Business Plan
- The Art of Bootstrapping
- The Art of Recruiting
- The Art of Raising Capital
- The Art of Partnering
- The Art of Branding
- The Art of Rainmaking
- The Art of Being a Mensch

## Key Principles

- Make meaning, not just money
- Jumpstart instead of planning
- Position yourself as unique
- Follow the 10/20/30 rule for pitches

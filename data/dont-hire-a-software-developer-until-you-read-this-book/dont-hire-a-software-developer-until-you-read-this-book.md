### Description

K.N. Kukoyi's book is a must-read for tech startup founders, providing crucial guidance on hiring software developers and managing tech projects. This practical guide offers insights into the intricacies of tech entrepreneurship and is designed to help founders make informed decisions when building their tech teams.

### Features

- **Comprehensive Hiring Guide:** The book provides a detailed roadmap for hiring the right software developers for your startup.
- **Project Management Tips:** Learn effective strategies for managing tech projects, ensuring they stay on track and within budget.
- **Entrepreneurial Insights:** Gain valuable knowledge on navigating the challenges of tech entrepreneurship, from conception to execution.
- **Practical Advice:** The book is filled with actionable advice and real-world examples to help you avoid common pitfalls.

## Overview

Obviously Awesome is a practical guide to product positioning that actually works, providing a systematic process for crafting powerful positioning.

## The 10-Step Positioning Process

1. Understand the customers who love your product
2. Form a positioning team
3. Align your positioning vocabulary
4. List your true competitive alternatives
5. Isolate your unique attributes
6. Map attributes to value
7. Determine who cares a lot
8. Find a market frame of reference
9. Layer on a trend
10. Capture your positioning

## Key Insights

- Positioning is context
- Features aren't benefits
- The best customers define positioning

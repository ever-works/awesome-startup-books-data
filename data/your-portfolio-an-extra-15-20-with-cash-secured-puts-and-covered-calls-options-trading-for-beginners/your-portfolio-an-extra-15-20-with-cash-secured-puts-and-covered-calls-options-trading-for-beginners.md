## Features

- **Beginner-oriented options guide**  
  - Written for readers new to options trading and derivatives.  
  - Positioned as part of a structured beginner series (*Options Trading for Beginners*).

- **Focus on the Options Wheel Strategy**  
  - Explains the “Options Wheel” as a repeatable approach to generating extra returns.  
  - Centered on two core option strategies:
    - **Cash-secured puts**  
    - **Covered calls**

- **Return enhancement concept**  
  - Aims to show how to target an additional ~15–20% return on an existing portfolio using options income strategies (not guaranteed performance, but the thematic goal of the book).

- **Portfolio and income orientation**  
  - Emphasizes boosting returns on a stock portfolio rather than short-term speculation.  
  - Relevant to personal finance and conservative–moderate options income approaches.

- **Series context**  
  - Labeled as **Book 4 of 6** in the *Options Trading for Beginners* series, allowing readers to explore related titles for broader coverage of options.

# Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies

Book by Nick Bostrom analyzing the potential development of superintelligent AI, associated risks, and strategic approaches to managing its impact on humanity. For startup founders, especially in tech and AI, it offers a deep framework for thinking about long-term consequences, ethics, and strategic positioning in advanced AI landscapes.

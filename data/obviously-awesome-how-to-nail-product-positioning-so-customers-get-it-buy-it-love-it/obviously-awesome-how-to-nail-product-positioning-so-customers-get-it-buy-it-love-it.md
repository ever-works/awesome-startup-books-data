## Overview

April Dunford provides a step-by-step process for positioning products so customers immediately understand their value. Based on 25 years launching products at startups, this is the practical positioning playbook.

## The 10-Step Positioning Process

1. Understand competitors
2. Isolate unique features
3. Map features to value
4. Identify best-fit customers  
5. Find the market you dominate
6. Layer on trend/themes
7. Capture mindshare with name
8. Test positioning
9. Roll out across company
10. Monitor and adjust

## Key Concepts

- Positioning = context for value
- You can't be everything to everyone
- Best customers see you as obviously awesome
- Position in a market you can dominate

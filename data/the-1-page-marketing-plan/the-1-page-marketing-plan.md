# The 1-Page Marketing Plan

A practical marketing book that helps founders and small-business owners create a concise, actionable marketing plan on a single page. It walks through a step‑by‑step framework covering lead generation, conversion, and customer retention, making it especially useful for startup founders who need a clear, executable go‑to‑market and customer acquisition strategy without complex corporate-style plans.

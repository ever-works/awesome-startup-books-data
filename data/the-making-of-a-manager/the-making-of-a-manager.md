# The Making of a Manager

The Making of a Manager provides first-time founders with practical leadership lessons on transitioning to team leadership, including hiring, feedback, and one-on-ones for strong team building. Key principles emphasize vulnerability, trust-building, and supporting team growth. Vs High Output Management, it offers a gentler, story-driven introduction ideal for early-stage leaders.

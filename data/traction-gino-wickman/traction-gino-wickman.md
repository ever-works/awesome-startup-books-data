# Traction

Gino Wickman introduces EOS tactics for founders to gain operational traction via vision/traction organizer, scaling teams with people analyzers and issue solving. Key metrics include scorecards and rocks completion rates for growth phases. Vs Blitzscaling, it builds structured processes for sustainable team scaling over chaotic speed.

## Overview

Getting Things Done (GTD) is a personal productivity system that enables stress-free productivity by capturing all commitments in a trusted external system.

## The Five Steps of GTD

1. **Capture**: Collect what has your attention
2. **Clarify**: Process what it means
3. **Organize**: Put it where it belongs
4. **Reflect**: Review frequently
5. **Engage**: Simply do

## Core Principles

- Your mind is for having ideas, not holding them
- Keep everything in a trusted system
- Weekly reviews maintain perspective
- Next actions make projects actionable

## Impact

GTD has become a standard productivity system used by millions worldwide.

## Overview

“Start. Scale. Exit. Repeat.” is a practical entrepreneurship playbook by serial founder Colin C. Campbell. Drawing on 30 years of startup experience, it walks founders through the full company lifecycle—starting, scaling, exiting, and then repeating the process—with an emphasis on actionable systems, frameworks, and real-world lessons.

---

## Features

### Core Focus & Structure
- Covers the **four main phases** of the entrepreneurial journey:
  - **Start** – turning ideas into viable, launch-ready businesses.
  - **Scale** – growing operations, teams, and revenue sustainably.
  - **Exit** – preparing for and executing a successful exit.
  - **Repeat** – applying lessons learned to build new ventures.
- Designed as a **structured blueprint** or **playbook** for founders at any stage.
- Emphasizes **practical insights**, **proven systems**, and **tactical guidance**, not just theory.

### Content Approach
- Uses **real-world stories** from Campbell’s repeated startup successes and failures.
- Focuses on **demystifying the startup journey** from idea to exit.
- Tailored to **entrepreneurs, small business owners, and startup founders** seeking repeatable processes.

### Author Background
- Written by **Colin C. Campbell**, a veteran entrepreneur, investor, and educator.
- Campbell has **built and exited multiple companies** over a 30-year career.
- Co-founder of **Startup Club**, a large entrepreneur community on Clubhouse.

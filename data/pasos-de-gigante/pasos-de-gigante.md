### Overview
“Pasos de gigante: La información que necesitas para triunfar en la vida y en los negocios, explicada de manera sencilla” is a Spanish-language business and self-improvement book. It focuses on practical guidance to help readers improve their mindset, motivation, and basic business skills. The content is aimed at people seeking foundational personal and professional development, including aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage founders, though its scope goes beyond startups.

### Features
- **Practical success framework** focused on succeeding both in life and in business.
- **Mindset development:** guidance on adopting a success-oriented, growth-focused mentality.
- **Motivation and habits:** tools and ideas to build motivation and sustain long-term effort.
- **Business fundamentals:** introductory concepts for people beginning in business or entrepreneurship.
- **Accessible explanations:** concepts are presented in simple, easy-to-understand language for a broad audience.
- **Applicable to multiple contexts:** relevant to personal goals, professional growth, and early-stage entrepreneurial projects.

### Audience
- Spanish-speaking readers interested in self-improvement and professional growth.
- Aspiring entrepreneurs and early-stage founders seeking basic business and mindset guidance.
- Individuals looking for an accessible introduction to personal development and business success principles.

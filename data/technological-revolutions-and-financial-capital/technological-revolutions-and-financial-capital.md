# Technological Revolutions and Financial Capital

Carlota Perez maps 50-year techno-economic cycles of installation (bubbles) and deployment (golden ages) for founders timing pivots with capital surges and innovation waves. Frameworks predict startup booms post-bust; long-cycle view vs Porter's short-term competition.

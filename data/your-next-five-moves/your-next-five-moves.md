## Overview
"Your Next Five Moves" is a strategy book for entrepreneurs, founders, and business-minded readers. It focuses on learning to think multiple moves ahead—similar to a grandmaster in chess—by combining business strategy, decision‑making frameworks, and personal development.

## Features

### Core Focus Areas
- **Strategic thinking:** Teaches readers to think several moves ahead in business and life, modeling the mindset of a grandmaster.
- **Entrepreneurship and startups:** Aimed at entrepreneurs and founders who want to start, build, and scale businesses.
- **Business strategy development:** Covers how to build a solid foundation for a business and how to level up or scale operations.
- **Personal and professional goal setting:** Applies strategic planning concepts to both career and personal life.
- **Decision‑making frameworks:** Provides structured, step‑by‑step approaches to choosing and sequencing key moves.

### Practical Approach
- **Actionable guidance:** Emphasizes practical advice and concrete steps that readers can implement, rather than abstract theory.
- **Step‑by‑step methodology:** Uses a sequential, process‑driven format to help readers clarify what they want, plan, and execute.
- **Tools for execution:** Offers tools and mental models to envision a better future and work systematically toward it.

### Learning Style & Structure
- **Readable, accessible style:** Written in an easy‑to‑understand way, suitable even for readers newer to business.
- **Story‑driven explanations:** Uses stories and experiences of successful people (including the author’s) to illustrate concepts.
- **Blend of mindset and skills:** Combines mindset shifts (how to think) with tactics and strategies (what to do).

### Use Cases
- Clarifying what you want out of life and business.
- Planning how to start a business from scratch.
- Strengthening and maintaining an existing business.
- Scaling a business in a structured, strategic way.
- Developing a long‑term strategic outlook on career and personal decisions.

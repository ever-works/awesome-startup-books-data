# Do More Faster: Techstars Lessons to Accelerate Your Startup

**Author:** Brad Feld, David G. Cohen  
**Published:** October 1, 2010  
**Format:** Kindle Edition  
**Pages:** 352  
**Category:** Startup Methodologies

## Description
"Do More Faster" is a book designed to offer pragmatic guidance and insights for startup founders. Authored by Brad Feld and David G. Cohen, it draws from the experiences and lessons learned within the Techstars accelerator program. The book acts as a roadmap for entrepreneurs navigating the early stages of their business journey, providing actionable advice and strategies to help their startups thrive.

## Features
- **Practical Advice:** Offers direct and useful tips for startup founders.
- **Techstars Insights:** Shares lessons learned from the Techstars accelerator program.
- **Early-Stage Guidance:** Focuses on navigating the initial phases of business development.
- **Startup Focus:** Targeted specifically towards entrepreneurs and startup enthusiasts.

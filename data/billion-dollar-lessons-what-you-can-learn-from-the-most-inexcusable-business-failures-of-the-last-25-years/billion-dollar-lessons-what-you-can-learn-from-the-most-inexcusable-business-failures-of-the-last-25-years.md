# Billion Dollar Lessons: What You Can Learn from the Most Inexcusable Business Failures of the Last 25 Years

A book that analyzes major corporate and startup failures over 25 years, extracting patterns and lessons founders can use to avoid strategic mistakes in areas such as acquisitions, scalability, and business model design.

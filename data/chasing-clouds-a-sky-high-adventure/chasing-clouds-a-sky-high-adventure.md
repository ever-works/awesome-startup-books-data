## Description

“Chasing Clouds: A Sky-High Adventure” is a children’s adventure book that follows a whimsical journey through the sky. Although presented as a playful story, it also introduces young readers to basic weather and science concepts such as the water cycle and different types of clouds, using simple language and engaging visuals.

- Introduces children to **the water cycle** in an accessible way.
- Teaches **different types of clouds**, such as cumulus, stratus, cirrus, and cumulonimbus.
- Helps explain **clouds and rain** through story-based learning.
- Designed to **educate while entertaining**, supporting both reading enjoyment and early science understanding.

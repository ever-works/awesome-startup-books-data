## Overview

Traction outlines strategies for startups to find effective ways to get customers using the Bullseye framework: brainstorm channels, prioritize, test traction, and focus.

- Publicity/PR
- Unconventional PR
- Paid search/SEM
- Social and display ads
- Offline ads
- SEO
- Content marketing
- Email marketing
- Engineering as marketing
- Viral marketing
- Business development
- Sales
- Affiliate programs
- Existing platforms
- Trade shows
- Offline events/seminars
- Speaking engagements
- Community building

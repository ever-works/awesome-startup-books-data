## Overview

Keith Ferrazzi shows how to build genuine relationships that create business opportunities. Networking as generosity, not transactions.

## Core Philosophy

- Give before you ask
- Build relationships before you need them
- Be genuinely interested in helping others
- Your network is your net worth

## Key Practices

- Follow up religiously
- Connect people to each other
- Share knowledge freely
- Remember personal details
- Create reasons to stay in touch

## The Ferrazzi Principles

1. Don't keep score
2. Be interesting by being interested
3. Build your brand
4. Create conferences and gatherings
5. Follow up or fail
6. Share your passions

## For Founders

- Investors are relationships
- Customers become advocates
- Employees refer talent
- Advisors open doors

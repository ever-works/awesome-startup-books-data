# Eat That Frog!

Productivity and time-management book by Brian Tracy that offers 21 practical methods to overcome procrastination and focus on high-value tasks. While not startup-specific, it is widely recommended for founders and entrepreneurs who need to prioritize effectively and execute more in less time, making it a useful supporting read for startup founders.

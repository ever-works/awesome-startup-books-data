## Overview

Traction presents a systematic approach to finding the marketing channels that will make your startup grow. The book introduces the Bullseye Framework for testing marketing channels.

14. Affiliate Programs
15. Existing Platforms
16. Trade Shows
17. Offline Events
18. Speaking Engagements
19. Community Building

## The Bullseye Framework

1. **Outer Ring**: Brainstorm all possible traction channels
2. **Middle Ring**: Test the most promising channels
3. **Inner Ring**: Focus on the channel that works best

## 50% Rule

Spend 50% of your time on product and 50% on traction.

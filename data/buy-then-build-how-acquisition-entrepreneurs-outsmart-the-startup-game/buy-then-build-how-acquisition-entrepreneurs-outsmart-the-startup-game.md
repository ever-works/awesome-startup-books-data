## Buy Then Build: How Acquisition Entrepreneurs Outsmart the Startup Game

A practical guide to **acquisition entrepreneurship**, focusing on buying and growing existing small businesses instead of starting companies from scratch.

### Features
- Focuses on the strategy of acquiring existing small businesses as a path to entrepreneurship.
- Explains how founders can potentially create more value by buying and improving companies rather than launching new startups.
- Covers the end-to-end acquisition process, including:
  - Sourcing potential deals
  - Structuring and securing financing
  - Conducting due diligence
  - Operating and growing acquired companies after purchase
- Oriented toward startup-minded founders and professionals exploring alternative paths to business ownership.

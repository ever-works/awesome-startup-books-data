## Ninja Innovation

![Ninja Innovation Cover](https://images.gr-assets.com/books/1356103791l/15818849.jpg)

### Overview
**Ninja Innovation** is a compelling guide authored by Gary Shapiro, offering strategic insights for businesses aiming to thrive in today's competitive economy. It provides a roadmap through ten distinctive strategies that have been successfully employed by some of the world's leading companies.

**Author:** Gary Shapiro  
**Publisher:** William Morrow Paperbacks  
**Publication Date:** January 8, 2013  
**Format:** Hardcover  
**Pages:** 256  
**Category:** Business Strategy  
**Tags:** Strategies, Competitive Economy, Success

### Features
- **Strategic Insights:** The book outlines ten killer strategies that have been pivotal for the success of top businesses around the globe.
- **Practical Guidance:** Designed as a resource for startup founders, offering practical advice and real-world applications.
- **Expert Author:** Authored by Gary Shapiro, a recognized figure in business strategy and innovation.

## Description
“The Three Rules” is a business strategy book that analyzes patterns from high-performing companies and distills them into three core rules for achieving superior performance. Aimed particularly at startup founders and business leaders, it offers evidence-based guidance on making strategic decisions that support sustainable, long-term growth rather than short-term gains.

## Features
- **Evidence-based approach:** Uses data and empirical analysis of high-performing companies rather than anecdotal or purely opinion-based advice.
- **Three core strategy rules:** Distills complex business performance patterns into three clear, actionable rules for superior performance (as described in the book).
- **Focus on sustainable growth:** Emphasizes strategies that drive long-term, durable performance instead of one-off wins.
- **Applicable to startups and established firms:** Tailored especially for startup founders, but relevant to leaders across various stages of company growth.
- **Decision-making framework:** Provides structured guidance for making strategic choices under uncertainty and competitive pressure.
- **Performance orientation:** Concentrates on what differentiates top-performing companies from average ones.
- **Accessible business writing:** Presents research-backed insights in a format intended for practitioners, not just academics.

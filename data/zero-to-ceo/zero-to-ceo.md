## Description
Zero to CEO is a startup-focused book that serves as a practical roadmap for turning an initial idea into a real company. Aimed at aspiring and current founders, it walks through the full startup journeyâ€”from validating a concept to building, launching, and scaling a business from scratch. The content is based on real-world experience and is designed to be applied directly to early-stage ventures.

## Features
- **End-to-end startup journey coverage**  
  Guides readers from the earliest stages of ideation through to building, launching, and scaling a company.

- **Focus on early validation**  
  Emphasizes validating ideas with real customers and ensuring there is genuine demand before committing significant resources.

- **Practical, experience-based guidance**  
  Provides actionable advice and frameworks grounded in startup experience rather than theory.

- **Roadmap for founders**  
  Structured as a step-by-step path that founders can follow as they progress from concept to an operational business.

- **For aspiring and active founders**  
  Written to be useful both for first-time entrepreneurs and for founders already working on a startup who want more structure.

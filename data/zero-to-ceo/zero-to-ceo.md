# Zero to CEO

**Category:** Biographies / Startup & Entrepreneurship  
**Brand / Publisher:** Not clearly specified

## Overview
Zero to CEO is a startup-focused book designed as a practical roadmap for turning an initial idea into a real company. Aimed at aspiring and current founders, it walks through the full journey from early validation to building, launching, and scaling a startup from scratch, based on real-world experience rather than theory.

## Features
- **End-to-end startup journey** – Covers the path from first idea to building, launching, and scaling a company.
- **Early idea validation** – Focuses on validating your concept before heavy investment in product or technology.
- **Practical, experience-based approach** – Emphasizes lessons drawn from real startup experience rather than abstract frameworks.
- **Roadmap for founders** – Structured as a step-by-step guide for both new and existing founders.
- **Startup execution focus** – Addresses the operational stages of creating and growing a startup from scratch.

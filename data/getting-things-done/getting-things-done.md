## Getting Things Done: The Art of Stress-Free Productivity

![Getting Things Done Cover Image](https://images.gr-assets.com/books/1395335634l/1633.jpg)

### Overview
**Getting Things Done** is a productivity methodology devised by David Allen. The approach is aimed at helping individuals and organizations enhance efficiency while reducing stress through effective time management and organizational techniques.

- **Author**: [David Allen](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/1058.David_Allen)
- **Publisher**: Penguin Books
- **Format**: Paperback, 267 pages
- **First Published**: January 1, 2001

### Features
- **Time Management Techniques**: Offers a systematic approach to managing tasks and commitments.
- **Stress Reduction**: Strategies to decrease stress by organizing tasks and thoughts.
- **Efficiency Boost**: Methods to increase personal and professional productivity.
- **Organization**: Techniques to structure information and responsibilities effectively.

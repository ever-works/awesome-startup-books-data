## Overview
“The Customer Rules” is a practical guide to customer experience and service excellence by Lee Cockerell, former Executive Vice President of Operations at Walt Disney World. It lays out clear, actionable rules for delighting customers, earning their trust, and building long-term loyalty—especially relevant for startup founders focused on product–market fit, retention, and referral-driven growth.

## Features
- **Rule-based framework:** Organized as a set of concrete “rules” that break down what great service looks like in everyday behavior.  
- **Focus on loyalty and retention:** Emphasizes how consistent service excellence drives repeat business, word-of-mouth, and long-term customer relationships.  
- **Customer-centric mindset:** Guides readers to view decisions, operations, and product choices through the lens of the customer experience.  
- **Practical, day-to-day tactics:** Concentrates on what employees, managers, and founders can do in daily interactions to improve satisfaction (e.g., responsiveness, clarity, reliability).  
- **People and culture oriented:** Highlights the link between how you treat your team and the service they provide to customers.  
- **Applicable across industries:** Lessons are framed broadly for use in startups, small businesses, and large organizations alike.  
- **Relevance for founders and operators:** Useful for designing support processes, onboarding flows, and product experiences that feel dependable and delightful.  
- **Retention and product–market fit link:** Connects strong customer experience to better adoption, engagement, and reduced churn.

## Ideal For
- Startup founders and early team members setting a customer-experience foundation.  
- Managers building a service-driven culture.  
- Anyone responsible for customer satisfaction, loyalty, and frontline service.

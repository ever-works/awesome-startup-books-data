## Overview

“Starting & Managing Your S Corp” is a small-business and startup-focused guide to forming, running, and maintaining an S Corporation. It emphasizes practical bookkeeping and accounting strategies designed to help founders reduce small business taxes while choosing the right corporate structure and setting up financial operations for a new or growing company.

## Features

- **S Corporation formation guidance**
  - Step-by-step explanation of how to form an S Corp.  
  - Oriented toward first-time founders and small-business owners.

- **Corporate structure decision support**
  - Helps compare S Corporations to other common business structures when deciding how to incorporate a startup or small business.

- **Operational management of an S Corp**
  - Guidance on running and maintaining an S Corporation after formation.  
  - Focus on compliance and ongoing management needs for small businesses.

- **Bookkeeping strategies**
  - Practical bookkeeping methods tailored to S Corporations.  
  - Aimed at creating clear financial records suitable for tax and regulatory purposes.

- **Accounting approaches for S Corps**
  - Accounting strategies designed for small businesses operating as S Corporations.  
  - Focus on organizing revenue, expenses, and distributions in an S Corp context.

- **Tax reduction focus**
  - Methods and frameworks intended to reduce small business tax burden within the rules that apply to S Corporations.  
  - Useful for founders structuring compensation, distributions, and expenses.

- **Startup-oriented guidance**
  - Written with early-stage founders and new business operators in mind.  
  - Aligns entity choice and financial setup with startup growth plans.

- **Financial operations setup**
  - Helps readers set up core financial operations for their startup or small business.  
  - Covers integrating bookkeeping, accounting, and corporate structure choices into a coherent system.

**Format:** 256 pages, Hardcover

## Description
"Start with Why" by Simon Sinek delves into the profound concept of understanding the "why" behind actions as a significant catalyst for leadership and innovation. This book is considered essential for startup founders and leaders aiming to inspire and guide their teams effectively. Sinek emphasizes the importance of a clear purpose in driving successful leadership and fostering innovation.

## Features
- **Exploration of "Why":** The book centers around identifying and understanding the core motivations that drive individuals and organizations.
- **Leadership Insights:** Provides guidance for leaders on how to inspire and motivate teams by focusing on purpose and vision.
- **Innovation Catalyst:** Offers strategies and real-world examples to spur innovation by aligning with a clear sense of "why."

# Why Startups Fail: A New Roadmap for Entrepreneurial Success

A startup-focused book by Tom Eisenmann that analyzes common reasons new ventures fail and provides a practical framework to diagnose risks, avoid typical pitfalls, and improve an early-stage company’s odds of success—highly relevant for founders seeking must‑read guidance on what goes wrong and how to prevent it.

## Overview

Michael Gerber shows why most small businesses fail: founders work IN the business instead of ON the business. Build systems, not jobs.

## The E-Myth

The Entrepreneurial Myth: being good at technical work means you can run a business doing that work. False.

## Three Personalities

- **Technician**: Does the work
- **Manager**: Plans and organizes
- **Entrepreneur**: Envisions the future

Most founders are 70% Technician. Need balance.

## The Turnkey Revolution

Build your business like a franchise prototype:
- Documented systems
- Repeatable processes
- Can run without you

## For Founders

- Work ON business, not IN it
- Document everything
- Create systems for every role
- Build to sell, even if you won't

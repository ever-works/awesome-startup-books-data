# The Startup CTO's Handbook

**Category:** Biographies  
**Author:** Zach Goldberg  
**Format:** Book (content also available as Markdown in the linked GitHub repository)

## Overview
The Startup CTO's Handbook is a practical guide for startup founders and technical leaders on building and scaling high-performing software engineering organizations. It focuses on the real-world responsibilities of a CTO in early-stage and growing startups, combining leadership, management, and technical topics for people leading engineering teams.

Source: [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/ZachGoldberg/Startup-CTO-Handbook)

## Features
- **CTO role in startups**
  - Explores the responsibilities and expectations of a CTO in early-stage technology companies.
  - Addresses how the CTO role evolves as a startup grows.

- **Leadership & management**
  - Guidance on leading software engineering teams.
  - Management practices tailored to high-growth startup environments.

- **Organizational design**
  - Approaches to structuring engineering teams.
  - Considerations for scaling team size and roles over time.

- **Hiring & team building**
  - Strategies for hiring engineers in startup contexts.
  - Building and nurturing high-performing teams.

- **Culture**
  - Creating and maintaining a healthy engineering culture.
  - Aligning engineering culture with company goals.

- **Technical decision-making**
  - Frameworks and guidance for making technology choices in startups.
  - Balancing speed, quality, and long-term maintainability.

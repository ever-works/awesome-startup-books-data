# Ingrain AI

A book focused on how organizations can design and execute an AI-first strategy, covering cultural change, operational playbooks, and practical guidance for scaling AI initiatives from concept to execution. It is relevant for startup founders building AI-native companies who need a structured approach to integrating AI into product, operations, and company culture.

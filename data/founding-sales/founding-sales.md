# Founding Sales

## Overview
Founding Sales by Peter Kazanjy demystifies the sales process for startup founders. It offers a detailed, tactical guide to founder-led sales, from pitching to closing deals and transitioning to a hired sales team.

## Features
- Step-by-step sales process for founders
- Sales pitch templates and scripts
- Objection handling techniques
- Follow-up and pipeline management strategies
- Guidance on hiring and managing your first sales reps
- Real-world examples and case studies

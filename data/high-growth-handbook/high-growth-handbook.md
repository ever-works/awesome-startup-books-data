## High Growth Handbook

**Author:** Elad Gil  
**Publication Date:** July 17, 2018  
**Format:** Hardcover, 344 pages

### Description
The "High Growth Handbook" by Elad Gil offers an in-depth exploration of scaling startups effectively. This guide is especially useful for founders aiming to expand their businesses from small teams to large organizations.

### Features
- **Comprehensive Guide:** Covers diverse topics essential for startup growth, including hiring strategies, product management, and organizational scaling.
- **Targeted Audience:** Aimed at founders and entrepreneurs who are planning rapid expansion.
- **Expert Insights:** Written by Elad Gil, a seasoned entrepreneur with experience in both building and investing in startups.
- **Practical Advice:** Offers actionable steps and practical advice for managing and scaling a growing company.

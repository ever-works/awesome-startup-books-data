## Overview

Michael Watkins provides a roadmap for leaders in transition—whether new CEO, joining a board, or taking on expanded responsibilities.

## The Framework

### First 30 Days
- Promote yourself mentally
- Accelerate your learning
- Match strategy to situation
- Secure early wins

### Days 30-60
- Negotiate success
- Achieve alignment
- Build your team

### Days 60-90
- Create coalitions
- Keep your balance
- Accelerate everyone

## STARS Model

- **Start-up**: Building from scratch
- **Turnaround**: Saving failing unit
- **Accelerated Growth**: Scaling fast
- **Realignment**: Fixing drift
- **Sustaining Success**: Maintaining momentum

Each requires different strategies.

## For Founders

- Applies to every new growth stage
- Transitioning from doer to leader
- Bringing on executives
- Role changes as company scales

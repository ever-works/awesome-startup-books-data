# Taking a Mexican Company Global—The CEMEX Way

A Harvard Business School case study about CEMEX’s global growth-through-acquisition strategy. While focused on corporate expansion, finance, and operations, it is a teaching case rather than a must‑read book for startup founders and does not belong in a core startup books directory.

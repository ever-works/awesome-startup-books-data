### Nail It Then Scale It

**Author:** Nathan Furr

**Co-author:** Paul Ahlstrom

**Publication Date:** January 1, 2010

**Format:** Paperback, 258 pages

**Category:** Growth & Scaling

**Tags:** Product-Market Fit, Scaling, Startup

**Description:**
"Nail It Then Scale It" is a practical guide for entrepreneurs and innovators aiming to launch successful startups. Written by Nathan Furr, this book emphasizes the importance of achieving the right product-market fit before scaling operations. It provides a structured approach to identifying and validating business ideas, ensuring they meet market needs and demands before expanding.

**Features:**
- **Step-by-Step Guidance:** Offers a detailed roadmap for entrepreneurs to follow, focusing on critical early stages of startup development.
- **Product-Market Fit:** Prioritizes identifying the right market fit to reduce risks and increase the chances of startup success.
- **Scaling Techniques:** Provides strategies on how to scale effectively once the product-market fit is achieved.
- **Real-World Examples:** Includes case studies and examples from successful startups to illustrate key concepts.

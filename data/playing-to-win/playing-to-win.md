## Overview

Playing to Win outlines the strategic approach A.G. Lafley used to turn around P&G, presenting a clear framework for making strategic choices that create sustainable competitive advantage.

## The Strategy Choice Cascade

1. **What is our winning aspiration?**
2. **Where will we play?**
3. **How will we win?**
4. **What capabilities must we have?**
5. **What management systems are required?**

## Key Principles

- Strategy is about making choices, not just setting goals
- You can't be everything to everyone
- Winning requires a clear competitive advantage
- Strategy must be testable and reversible

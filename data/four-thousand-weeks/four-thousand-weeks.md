## Overview

Four Thousand Weeks challenges conventional productivity advice by embracing life's limitations and finitude.

## Core Philosophy

The average human lifespan is about 4,000 weeks. Rather than trying to do everything, accept limitations and focus on what truly matters.

## Key Insights

- You'll never get everything done
- FOMO is inevitable - embrace it
- Efficiency traps create more work
- Pay yourself first (with time)
- Accept imperfection

## For Entrepreneurs

Helps founders make peace with limited time and focus on highest-impact activities.

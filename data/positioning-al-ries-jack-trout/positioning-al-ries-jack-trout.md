## Overview

Positioning is the first body of thought to come to grips with the problems of communicating to an overcommunicated society.

## Core Concept

Positioning is not what you do to a product. Positioning is what you do to the mind of the prospect.

## Key Principles

- Own a word in the prospect's mind
- Be first in a category
- Find the gap in the marketplace
- Reposition competitors
- Use the power of the negative

## Classic Examples

- Avis: "We're #2, We Try Harder"
- 7-Up: "The Uncola"
- Volkswagen: "Think Small"

## Impact

Revolutionized marketing and advertising, remains essential 50 years later.

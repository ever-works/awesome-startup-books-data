## My Life and Work: Autobiography of Henry Ford

**Category:** Biographies  
**Tags:** Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Leadership  

**Description:**
"My Life and Work" is an autobiography penned by the iconic industrialist Henry Ford. In this classic account, Ford shares his journey and insights into the world of entrepreneurship, innovation, and leadership. This book is an essential read for anyone interested in understanding the foundational principles of building and managing a successful business.

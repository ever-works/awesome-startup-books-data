## Burn Rate: Launching a Startup and Losing My Mind

**Author:** Andy Dunn  
**Category:** Startup Life & Culture  
**Tags:** Mental Health, Scaling, Entrepreneurship

### Description
"Burn Rate: Launching a Startup and Losing My Mind" is a gripping memoir by Andy Dunn, the former CEO of Bonobos. This national best-seller takes readers on a raw and insightful journey through the tumultuous world of launching and scaling a startup. Dunn candidly shares the personal and professional challenges he faced, highlighting the often-overlooked intersection between entrepreneurship and mental health.

### Key Themes
- **Entrepreneurial Challenges:** Explores the intense pressures and hurdles encountered while scaling a business.
- **Mental Health:** Offers a poignant look at the mental health struggles that can accompany the entrepreneurial journey.
- **Personal Narrative:** Provides an intimate account of Dunn's experiences and the toll they took on his personal life.

### FAQs
- **Why Does Burn Rate Matter?**
  - It measures how quickly your business is losing money, crucial for determining how long you can continue operations without new revenue.
- **How to Calculate Burn Rate?**
  - Formula: (Cash at Start of Period - Cash at End of Period) / Number of Months
- **How to Calculate Gross Burn Rate?**
  - Formula: (Monthly Operating Expenses / Starting Cash Available) * 100

### "Who" - Book Summary

**Title:** Who: The A Method for Hiring  
**Authors:** Geoff Smart and Randy Street  
**Category:** Leadership & Management  
**Tags:** Hiring, Team-Building, Leadership

**Description:**
"Who" is a compelling guide for startups and business leaders focused on refining their hiring process. Authored by Geoff Smart and Randy Street, the book introduces a structured 4-step method to enhance the hiring process, emphasizing the selection of the right team members. Through insightful interviews with high-profile leaders, "Who" serves as a valuable resource to help businesses avoid costly hiring mistakes.

**Features:**
- **4-Step Hiring Method:** A systematic approach to hiring that includes defining the role, sourcing candidates, selecting the right fit, and integrating new hires.
- **Leader Interviews:** Insights and experiences shared by high-profile leaders on successful hiring practices.
- **Costly Mistake Prevention:** Strategies to avoid common pitfalls in the hiring process, saving businesses time and resources.
- **Team Building:** Emphasizes the importance of assembling a team that aligns with the company's vision and goals.

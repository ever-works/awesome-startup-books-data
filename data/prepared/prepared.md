## Overview

*Prepared* is a book about long-term planning, resilience, and readiness. It encourages readers—especially founders and organizational leaders—to look beyond short-term growth and think about how to build durable, crisis-ready organizations that can adapt and thrive over time.

## Features

- Explores principles of long-term planning and preparedness.  
- Focuses on resilience at the individual and organizational levels.  
- Offers a readiness-focused mindset, emphasizing how to anticipate and navigate crises.  
- Aims to shift attention from short-term wins to building sustainable, durable structures and cultures.  
- Relevant for founders and leaders who want to design organizations capable of withstanding uncertainty and change.

## Use Cases

- Founders seeking frameworks for building long-term, crisis-ready companies.  
- Leaders looking to strengthen resilience and preparedness in their teams or organizations.  
- Readers interested in decision-making and strategy with an emphasis on sustainability over short-term gains.

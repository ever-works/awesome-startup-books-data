## Overview

Range challenges the cult of hyper-specialization, showing how generalists often outperform specialists in our complex world.

## Key Arguments

- Late specialization can be an advantage
- Sampling period builds judgment
- Analogical thinking solves novel problems
- Match quality improves with exploration

## The Wicked World

In "wicked" learning environments:
- Rules aren't clear
- Feedback is delayed or unclear
- Patterns repeat less  
- Breadth of experience helps

## For Entrepreneurs

Founders benefit from diverse experiences that enable pattern recognition across domains.

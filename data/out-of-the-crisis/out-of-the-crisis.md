## Overview

Out of the Crisis is a seminal work on management and quality control by W. Edwards Deming, a pioneer in the field.

## Key Insights from Recommender

- "Big or small, this book focuses the entrepreneur/manager on respecting employees, focusing on process, and insisting on the collection and analysis of data." – Roger Ehrenberg, founder and managing partner of IA Ventures

## Relevance to Startups

The book is described as critical for startup founders, providing principles that help in building respectful, process-driven, and data-informed organizations.

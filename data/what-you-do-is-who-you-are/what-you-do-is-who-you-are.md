## Overview

Ben Horowitz explores how to build and maintain company culture by studying historical figures from Genghis Khan to Toussaint Louverture.

## Key Principles

**Culture is not what you say, it's what you do**

Actions, not values statements, create culture.

## Building Culture

1. Create shocking rules
2. Incorporate outside leadership
3. Make decisions that demonstrate priorities
4. Walk the talk
5. Make ethics explicit

## Historical Lessons

- Genghis Khan: Meritocracy and loyalty
- Samurai: Bushido code
- Haiti's revolution: Fighting for principles
- Shaka Senghor: Prison transformation

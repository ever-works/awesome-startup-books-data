# Micromax: Scaling the Largest Indian Mobile Handset Company

- **Type:** Harvard Business School case study (Case #415034)
- **Category:** Growth & Scaling
- **Brand / Publisher:** Harvard Business School Publishing
- **Topic Focus:** Hypergrowth, scaling an emerging-market consumer electronics company, leadership transitions
- **Source:** [Official HBS item page](https://www.hbs.edu/faculty/Pages/item.aspx?num=48653)

## Overview
This case study examines how Micromax scaled from a **$54 million** business to over **$1 billion in revenue in six years**, becoming India’s largest mobile handset company. It explores the strategic, organizational, and leadership challenges that arise when a founder-led startup grows into a large, professionally managed company under conditions of rapid market expansion.

## Features
- **Growth Narrative**  
  - Tracks Micromax’s trajectory from a relatively small player to India’s largest mobile handset company.  
  - Highlights revenue growth from **$54 million to $1+ billion** in about six years.  
  - Situates the company within India’s fast-growing mobile and consumer electronics market.

- **Founder vs. Professional Management**  
  - Explores tensions between founder-driven decision-making and the need for professional managers.  
  - Raises questions about when and how to bring in professional leadership.  
  - Considers how founders can evolve their roles as the company scales.

- **Organizational Growing Pains**  
  - Examines structural and process challenges created by hypergrowth.  
  - Looks at how rapid expansion stresses systems, culture, and communication.  
  - Surfaces issues in aligning rapidly expanding teams with company strategy.

- **Strategic Leadership Under Hypergrowth**  
  - Focuses on strategic choices required to sustain growth at scale.  
  - Considers trade-offs in product, market, and organizational strategy during rapid expansion.  
  - Raises questions of governance, decision rights, and leadership style in a high-velocity environment.

- **Relevance for Startup Founders & Leaders**  
  - Designed for founders and executives facing scale-up challenges.  
  - Provides a real-world example of leadership transition in a high-growth, emerging-market context.  
  - Useful for courses or workshops on scaling, leadership, and organizational design.

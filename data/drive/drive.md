**Author:** [Daniel H. Pink](https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/96150.Daniel_H_Pink)

**First Published:** December 29, 2009

**Format:** 242 pages, Hardcover

**Category:** Personal Development

**Tags:** Motivation, Intrinsic, Success

## Overview

*Drive* by Daniel H. Pink explores the science behind motivation, challenging the traditional view that external rewards and punishments are the primary drivers of human behavior. Pink presents a compelling argument for the effectiveness of intrinsic motivators in fostering genuine engagement and success.

## Features

- **In-depth Analysis:** The book meticulously examines research on motivation, offering insights into why traditional incentive systems often fail to inspire creativity and productivity.
  
- **Focus on Intrinsic Motivation:** Highlights the power of autonomy, mastery, and purpose as key components that drive human motivation, especially in the modern workplace.
  
- **Applicable to Entrepreneurs:** Provides valuable guidance for startup founders and business leaders aiming to cultivate a motivated workforce.

- **Case Studies:** Rich with real-world examples and case studies that demonstrate the practical application of intrinsic motivators across various industries.

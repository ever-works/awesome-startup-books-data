# Hacking Growth

## Overview
Hacking Growth by Sean Ellis and Morgan Brown provides a framework for startups to achieve rapid, sustainable growth through experimentation and data-driven processes. It covers optimizing the entire customer lifecycle.

## Features
- Growth hacking process and methodology
- Techniques for optimizing customer acquisition
- Strategies for improving retention and monetization
- Building cross-functional growth teams
- Real-world case studies from companies like LinkedIn, Airbnb, and Uber
- Ethical considerations and sustainable growth practices

# Go Big or Go Home

**Category:** Growth & Scaling  
**Type:** Book (Business / Startups)  
**Author:** Wil Schroter  
**ISBN:** 9781599712741  
**Brand / Seller:** Amazon  
**Slug:** `go-big-or-go-home`

A startup and entrepreneurship book focused on building large, scalable businesses without relying on outside funding. It emphasizes mindset, strategy, and hands-on tactics for founders who want to create high‑impact, high‑revenue companies through bootstrapping and disciplined growth instead of venture capital.

## Features

- Focus on **bootstrapping** rather than venture capital or outside funding.
- Emphasis on building **large, scalable businesses** from the outset.
- Guidance on developing a **“think big” founder mindset** while maintaining discipline.
- Strategies for **rapid, sustainable growth** without sacrificing control.
- Tactics for creating **high‑impact, high‑revenue companies**.
- Discussion of **lean operations and disciplined growth** practices.
- Targeted at **founders and early‑stage entrepreneurs**.
- Suitable for those who want to **avoid dilution and maintain ownership** while scaling.

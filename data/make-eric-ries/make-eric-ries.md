## Overview

The Maker Movement Manifesto explores how the democratization of manufacturing tools is enabling a new generation of entrepreneurs.

## The Maker Movement Principles

- Make
- Share
- Give
- Learn
- Tool up
- Play
- Participate
- Support
- Change

## Impact on Startups

- Lower barriers to prototyping
- Faster product development
- Access to manufacturing
- Community-driven innovation

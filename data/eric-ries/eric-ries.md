# Eric Ries

**Category:** Startup Methodologies  
**Tags:** lean, startup, innovation  
**Website:** https://www.goodreads.com/author/show/140632.Eric_Ries

## Overview
Eric Ries is an author and thinker on startup methodology, best known for developing and popularizing the Lean Startup approach. His work focuses on applying lean principles, rapid experimentation, and validated learning to help founders systematically innovate, build products, and scale startups.

## Features
- **Lean Startup Framework**
  - Introduces a structured methodology for building startups under conditions of extreme uncertainty.
  - Emphasizes efficiency and learning over traditional long-term planning.

- **Build–Measure–Learn Loop**
  - Core iterative cycle for product development and improvement.
  - Encourages quickly building a minimum version of a product, measuring user response, and learning from data to guide next steps.

- **Validated Learning**
  - Focuses on testing hypotheses about customers and products with real-world experiments.
  - Uses evidence and metrics rather than assumptions to drive product decisions.

- **Lean Principles Applied to Startups**
  - Adapts concepts from lean manufacturing to innovation and entrepreneurship.
  - Aims to reduce waste in product development by avoiding building features customers do not want.

- **Systematic Approach to Innovation**
  - Provides a repeatable process for discovering viable products and business models.
  - Helps teams align around clear goals, metrics, and learning milestones.

## Notable Work

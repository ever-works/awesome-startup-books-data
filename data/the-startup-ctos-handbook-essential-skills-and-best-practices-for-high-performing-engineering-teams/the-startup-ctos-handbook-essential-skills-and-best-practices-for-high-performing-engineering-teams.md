# The Startup CTO's Handbook: Essential skills and best practices for high performing engineering teams

A practical guide for startup founders and technical leaders on how to build, grow, and manage high-performing engineering teams. It covers the essential skills, processes, and best practices a CTO needs in an early-stage and scaling startup, including hiring, culture, architecture decisions, and collaboration with the broader founding team.

# Choose Yourself!

A personal and entrepreneurial development book by James Altucher that argues founders must stop waiting for external validation—investors, employers, or gatekeepers—and instead build their own opportunities through experimentation, side projects, and resilient habits, making it a motivational read for early-stage startup founders.

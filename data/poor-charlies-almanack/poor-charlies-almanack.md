## Description
A curated collection of speeches, essays, and commentary from Berkshire Hathaway vice chairman Charlie Munger. While not startup‑specific, it’s widely recommended for founders for its focus on mental models, multidisciplinary thinking, decision‑making frameworks, and long‑term business philosophy that supports sound entrepreneurial judgment.

## Features
- Collection of Charlie Munger’s speeches, essays, and commentary.
- Emphasis on lifelong learning and constant reading as a foundation for wisdom and good judgment.
- Exploration of mental models drawn from multiple disciplines (e.g., psychology, economics, mathematics, history).
- Practical decision‑making frameworks aimed at reducing errors and bias.
- Long‑term, value‑oriented business philosophy rooted in Berkshire Hathaway’s approach.
- Applicable to founders and entrepreneurs seeking to improve strategic thinking and judgment.

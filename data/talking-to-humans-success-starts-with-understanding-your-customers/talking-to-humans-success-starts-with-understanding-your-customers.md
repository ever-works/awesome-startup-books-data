## Overview

Giff Constable provides a short, practical guide to customer development interviews. Learn what to ask, who to talk to, and how to synthesize insights.

## Key Topics

- Who to talk to
- How to find them
- Questions that reveal truth
- Taking notes effectively
- Synthesizing what you learned
- When you've learned enough

## The Framework

### Before Interviews
- Define assumptions to test
- Create discussion guide
- Recruit diverse participants

### During Interviews
- Ask about past behavior
- Dig into specifics
- Watch for workarounds
- Listen more than talk

### After Interviews
- Look for patterns
- Challenge your assumptions
- Decide what to build (or not)

## Best Practices

- 5-10 interviews per cohort
- Interview until patterns emerge
- Don't pitch—learn
- Record (with permission)
- Share insights with team

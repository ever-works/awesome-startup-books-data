# Amazon Storefront – Startup Books Collection

## Overview
A curated Amazon storefront page featuring a collection of startup-related books aimed at founders, entrepreneurs, and early-stage teams. The page aggregates multiple titles into a single place on Amazon, making it easier to browse and purchase influential books on building and scaling startups.

## Features
- **Curated startup book selection**: Focuses on must-read titles for startup founders and entrepreneurs (e.g., books in the vein of *Zero to One* and *The Lean Startup*).
- **Single storefront page**: Consolidates multiple relevant books into one Amazon collection page for streamlined browsing.
- **Direct purchasing on Amazon**: Each listed book links to its individual Amazon product page for purchase.
- **Amazon ecosystem integration**:
  - Available through the main Amazon website.
  - Supports standard Amazon account features such as wish lists, order history, and recommendations.
- **Format variety (per book)**: Individual titles may be available in different formats (e.g., Kindle, paperback, hardcover, audiobook) depending on each product listing.
- **Discoverability**: Accessible via Amazon’s search and navigation, and can be added to user lists or carts like any other Amazon product.

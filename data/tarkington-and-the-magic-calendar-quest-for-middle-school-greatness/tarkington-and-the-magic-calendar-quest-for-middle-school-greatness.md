# Tarkington and the Magic Calendar: Quest for Middle School Greatness

## Overview
“Roger Tarkington and the Magic Calendar: Quest for Middle School Greatness” is a middle-grade fiction book about a boy who discovers a magical calendar that helps him navigate the challenges of middle school. The story focuses on youth, personal growth, and school life, with light science-fiction elements.

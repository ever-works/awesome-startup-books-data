## Overview

Noam Wasserman studied 10,000 founders to identify the key decisions that make or break startups. Data-driven insights on founder dilemmas.

## The Core Dilemmas

### Career Dilemmas
- When to found?
- Solo or co-founder?
- With whom?

### Relationship Dilemmas
- How to split equity?
- Roles and titles?
- Decision-making authority?

### Investor Dilemmas
- Angel or VC?
- How much control to cede?
- Board composition?

## Key Findings

- Equal splits often fail
- Hire slow, fire fast with co-founders
- Vesting is essential
- Friends/family are risky co-founders
- Rich vs. King: choose one

## Rich vs. King

You can't optimize for both:
- **Rich**: Maximize financial outcome (take money, give up control)
- **King**: Maintain control (bootstrap, slower growth)

## For Founders

- Make hard decisions early
- Use vesting always
- Document everything
- Anticipate dilemmas before they hit

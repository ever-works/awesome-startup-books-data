## Overview

William Poundstone explores the hidden psychology of value, showing how prices are arbitrary anchors that businesses can influence.

## Key Insights

- There is no "fair" price
- Anchoring determines value perception
- Context changes willingness-to-pay
- Price endings matter ($99 vs $100)
- Decoy pricing works

## Pricing Tactics

- **Anchoring**: Show expensive option first
- **Decoys**: Add option that makes target look better
- **Price Ending**: .99 vs .00 psychology
- **Bundling**: Combine to obscure individual prices
- **Tiering**: Good-better-best options

## For Product Pricing

- Price relative to anchors
- Create price-quality associations
- Use strategic comparisons
- Test extensively

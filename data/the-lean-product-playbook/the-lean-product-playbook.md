**Author:** Dan Olsen  
**Publisher:** Wiley  
**Category:** Startup Methodologies  

### Description
_The Lean Product Playbook_ is a comprehensive guide for startup founders and product managers on how to effectively apply lean principles to the product development process. Authored by Dan Olsen, this book emphasizes the importance of creating products that truly meet customer needs through the use of Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) and rapid customer feedback.

### Features
- **Lean Principles:** Learn how to apply lean methodologies to streamline product development and minimize waste.
- **Minimum Viable Products (MVPs):** Guidance on creating MVPs to validate product ideas quickly and efficiently.
- **Customer Feedback:** Techniques for gathering and integrating customer feedback into the product development cycle.
- **Product/Market Fit:** Strategies for achieving product/market fit to ensure your product meets the demands of the target audience.
- **Iterative Development:** Insights into building products iteratively, allowing for continuous improvement and adaptation.

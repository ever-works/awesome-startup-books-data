### Description
"Lean Analytics" is a comprehensive guide that underscores the significance of analytics in the startup ecosystem. Authored by Alistair Croll and Benjamin Yoskovitz, the book provides a data-driven framework for entrepreneurs and intrapreneurs to validate problems, identify the right customer base, and determine optimal strategies for building, monetizing, and marketing their products.

### Features
- **Data-Driven Approach:** Focuses on the use of analytics for informed decision-making in startups.
- **One Metric That Matters:** Encourages startups to concentrate on the most critical metric at each stage of growth.
- **Practical Steps:** Offers over 30 case studies and insights from interviews with over a hundred founders and investors.
- **Lean Startup Principles:** Builds on the foundational concepts of the Lean Startup methodology.

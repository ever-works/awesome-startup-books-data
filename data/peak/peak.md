## Overview

"Peak" by Chip Conley applies Maslow's hierarchy of needs to business, focusing on how companies can meet hierarchical human needs to drive performance.

## Key Insights from Recommender

- Described as "Maslow’s hierarchy of needs adapted to the business world."
- Provides "sound advice on achieving ‘sustainable outperformance’ and leveraging crises for the better." – Fred Destin, partner at Accel Partners

## Relevance to Startups

The book helps startup founders think about organizational needs and employee/customer motivation in a structured way, useful for building resilient and high‑performing companies.

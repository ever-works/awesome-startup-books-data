## Overview
“Anything You Want” is a concise, story-driven book by Derek Sivers, distilling 40 lessons he learned from building and selling CD Baby. The book focuses on unconventional, founder-centric entrepreneurship and building a business that reflects your personal values rather than standard startup playbooks.

---

## Features
- **Entrepreneurship lessons from CD Baby**  
  Draws on Sivers’ real-world experience starting, growing, and selling his independent music company CD Baby.

- **40 short, focused chapters**  
  Each lesson is presented as a compact story or idea, making the book easy to dip into and reference.

- **Founder-centric approach**  
  Encourages building a company around the founder’s personality, preferences, and values instead of copying Silicon Valley norms.

- **Emphasis on customer obsession**  
  Highlights designing products, services, and processes around what truly serves and delights customers.

- **Values-driven business building**  
  Advocates creating startups that align with your personal goals, ethics, and desired lifestyle.

- **Alternative to traditional startup metrics**  
  Questions the primacy of growth-at-all-costs, fundraising, and vanity metrics, favoring meaning, impact, and sustainability.

- **Story-based teaching style**  
  Lessons are conveyed primarily through personal anecdotes and narratives rather than abstract theory.

- **Suitable for aspiring and early-stage founders**  
  Aimed at people interested in launching or reshaping a startup around what matters most to them.

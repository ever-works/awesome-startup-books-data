# Core Curriculum Reading: Experimenting in the Entrepreneurial Venture

A Harvard Business School core reading (by Thomas R. Eisenmann, Eric Ries, and Sarah Dillard) that introduces hypothesis-driven entrepreneurship and lean startup experimentation. It teaches founders how to translate vision into testable business model hypotheses, design experiments, and use data to iterate toward product–market fit—making it a must-read foundational text for startup founders.

## Overview

Season of the Witch explores San Francisco's evolution from the 1950s to the 1980s, highlighting the cultural forces behind its creative energy.

## Key Themes

- Historical portrait of the city's transformation into an innovation hub
- Importance of cultural backdrop for markets, technology, and product development
- Lessons on how creativity flourishes in supportive environments

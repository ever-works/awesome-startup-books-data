## Overview

Super Founders analyzes 30,000+ data points from billion-dollar startups to reveal what really matters for success, challenging conventional wisdom.

## Key Findings

**Debunked Myths**:
- You don't need to drop out of college
- You don't need to start young
- You don't need prior startup experience
- Being first to market doesn't guarantee success

**What Actually Matters**:
- Market timing
- Founder-market fit
- Execution quality
- Willingness to pivot
- Building in growth markets

## Data-Driven Insights

Based on analysis of:
- Uber, Airbnb, Slack founders
- Their backgrounds, decisions, strategies
- Patterns that separate winners from losers

## Overview
“Never Wrestle with a Pig” is a collection of business advice and leadership lessons focused on handling difficult people, organizational politics, and conflict. It is particularly relevant for startup founders and leaders managing early-stage teams, partners, and diverse stakeholders.

## Features
- **Focus on difficult people:** Practical guidance on recognizing and managing challenging personalities in professional settings.
- **Politics and power dynamics:** Insights into navigating office and stakeholder politics without losing credibility or control.
- **Conflict management:** Strategies for defusing conflict, protecting relationships, and keeping work moving forward.
- **Leadership lessons:** Emphasis on real-world leadership behavior, decision-making, and how leaders show up under stress.
- **Communication skills:** Advice on framing conversations, setting boundaries, and getting your message across clearly.
- **Startup relevance:** Tailored value for startup founders and early leaders working with co-founders, investors, early employees, and partners.
- **Practical, situational tips:** Oriented around everyday scenarios rather than abstract theory.

### School for Startups

**Author:** Jim Beach  
**Category:** Startup Methodologies  
**Tags:** Small Business, Launch, Growth

---

#### Description
"School for Startups" is a comprehensive guide authored by Jim Beach that offers aspiring entrepreneurs a structured approach to achieving small business success swiftly. The book is designed to help individuals launch and grow their startups in just 90 days. 

#### Features
- **Structured Approach:** Provides a clear roadmap for launching a startup in 90 days.
- **Growth Strategies:** Offers insights into effective growth strategies to ensure business success.
- **Entrepreneurial Guidance:** Tailored advice for small business owners and startup enthusiasts.

# How the Hell Are You Doing This?

A leadership and personal development book by Parker McCumber that shares battle-tested strategies for taking control of your future, framed through the story of Captain Parker McCumber. While not exclusively about startups, it offers practical lessons on leadership, resilience, and success that are highly applicable for startup founders building and leading teams under pressure.

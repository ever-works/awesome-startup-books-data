## Overview

“The Great Game of Business” is a classic book on open-book management. It explains how to involve employees directly in understanding and improving a company’s financial performance. The book is especially relevant for startup founders and growing companies that want to build transparent, incentive-aligned operating systems as they scale.

## Features

- Explains the principles of **open-book management**: sharing financial information broadly within a company.  
- Focuses on **engaging employees** in financial performance and day-to-day business outcomes.  
- Offers guidance for **designing transparent operating systems** as organizations grow.  
- Emphasizes **incentive alignment**, helping leaders link employee rewards to company results.  
- Relevant for **startup founders** and leaders building participatory management cultures.  
- Applies to companies moving from small teams to **larger, more structured organizations**.

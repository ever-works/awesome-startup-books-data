## Overview

"Extreme Programming Explained" introduces Extreme Programming (XP), an agile software development methodology aimed at improving software quality and responsiveness to changing customer requirements.

## Relevance to Startups

For startup founders involved in product development, XP offers practices like test‑driven development, continuous integration, and frequent releases that can help build software efficiently and adapt to market feedback.

## Move Fast and Fix Things

**Authors:** Frances Frei and Anne Morriss  
**Category:** Leadership & Management  
**Tags:** Problem-Solving, Trust, Empowerment

### Description
"Move Fast and Fix Things" by Frances Frei and Anne Morriss is a compelling guide for leaders striving for swift problem-solving without sacrificing trust or excellence. The book disputes the common belief that companies must choose between innovation and caring for their workforce. It offers actionable strategies for fostering a culture of trust and empowerment within organizations.

### Features
- **Practical Problem-Solving:** Learn to identify and address the root of problems efficiently.
- **Building Trust:** Strategies to solve issues while maintaining and cultivating trust among team members.
- **Empowerment Techniques:** Methods to empower and engage employees, enhancing overall organizational performance.
- **Balancing Innovation & Care:** Redefines the approach to innovation, ensuring it does not come at the expense of employee welfare.

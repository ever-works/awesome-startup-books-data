## The Greater Good: Social Entrepreneurship for Everyday People Who Want to Change the World

**Author:** Madeleine Shaw

**Category:** Social Entrepreneurship

**Tags:** Social Impact, Entrepreneurship, Women

---

### Overview

*The Greater Good* by Madeleine Shaw is a compelling guide aimed at aspiring social entrepreneurs, particularly women and those traditionally marginalized in the business world. It serves as an inspirational resource that draws from Shaw's personal journey and the experiences of other successful social entrepreneurs, offering insights and motivation for readers who aspire to create impactful ventures.

### Features
- **Empowerment for Women:** The book emphasizes the role of women in social entrepreneurship, encouraging them to step into leadership roles and effect meaningful change.
- **Inclusivity:** Focuses on individuals who are often excluded from mainstream business, promoting diversity in entrepreneurial ventures.
- **Real-Life Examples:** Shaw shares stories and lessons from her own experiences as well as those of other social entrepreneurs, providing practical insights and strategies.
- **Actionable Advice:** Offers guidance on how to start and sustain social enterprises that can make a difference in communities and beyond.

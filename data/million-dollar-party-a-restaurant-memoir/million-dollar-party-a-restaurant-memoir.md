# Million Dollar Party: A Restaurant Memoir

Robert Mark recounts bootstrapping a thriving restaurant chain, detailing operations, customer service, and scaling in hospitality amid economic pressures. Founders learn hands-on tactics for team motivation, cost control, and word-of-mouth growth while avoiding overexpansion errors. Vs broader tech bios like Elon Musk, it offers grounded service-industry insights; great for non-tech inspiration and mistake avoidance.

# Competitive Advantage (Core Curriculum Reading in Strategy)

A Harvard Business School Core Curriculum Reading that introduces foundational concepts of competitive advantage—value creation and distribution, added value, willingness to pay, and supplier opportunity cost—and connects a firm’s unique activities to establishing and analyzing competitive advantage and value propositions, highly relevant for startup founders seeking to build durable strategic moats.

## Overview

Crossing the Chasm is a seminal work on technology adoption and market segmentation, introducing the concept of the "chasm" between early adopters and the early majority.

## Key Concepts

- Technology adoption lifecycle model
- Early adopters vs. early majority
- Strategies for crossing the chasm
- Importance of targeting a niche market
- Whole product concept

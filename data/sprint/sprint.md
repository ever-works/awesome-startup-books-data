## Sprint: How to Solve Big Problems and Test New Ideas in Just Five Days

**Author:** Jake Knapp

**Category:** Disruption & Innovation

**Tags:** Innovation, Process, Problem-Solving

**Description:**
"Sprint" by Jake Knapp presents a unique five-day process designed to help teams solve complex problems and test new ideas efficiently. This guide is particularly valuable for startup teams eager to innovate rapidly and effectively.

**Features:**
- **Five-Day Process:** The book outlines a structured approach that spans five days, guiding teams from problem identification to idea testing.
- **Practical Guidance:** Offers actionable steps that can be applied directly to real-world scenarios, helping teams to quickly iterate and improve.
- **Team Collaboration:** Focuses on enhancing teamwork and collaboration to drive innovation and problem-solving.
- **Rapid Prototyping:** Encourages creating prototypes swiftly to gather feedback and refine ideas.

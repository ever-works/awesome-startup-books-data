## Description
**"Quit"** by Annie Duke is a compelling exploration into the strategic significance of knowing when to quit. Aimed at startup founders and entrepreneurs, the book provides critical insights into decision-making and risk management, emphasizing the importance of strategic exits to optimize success and minimize losses.

## Features
- **Insightful Lessons:** Offers valuable lessons for startup founders on making informed decisions about when to persevere and when to pivot or exit.
- **Risk Management Strategies:** Provides frameworks for assessing risk and making calculated decisions to protect and grow your business.
- **Expert Analysis:** Draws on Annie Duke's extensive experience and insight to deliver practical advice on strategic quitting.

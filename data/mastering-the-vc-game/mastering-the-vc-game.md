# Mastering the VC Game

**Author:** Jeffrey Bussgang  
**Category:** Venture Capital / Startup Funding  
**Format:** Book  

## Overview
“Mastering the VC Game: A Venture Capital Insider Reveals How to Get from Start-up to IPO on Your Terms” is a founder-focused guide to navigating venture capital. Drawing on Jeffrey Bussgang’s experience as both a venture capitalist and an entrepreneur seeking funding, the book explains how to raise money without losing control of your company, negotiate favorable terms, and build productive investor relationships from startup stage through IPO.

## Features
- **Founder-centric perspective**
  - Explains common pitfalls startups face after taking venture capital, particularly loss of control to investors.
  - Shows how to structure the fundraising process so founders can maintain influence over key company decisions.

- **Balancing control and capital**
  - Discusses how venture funding can shift control dynamics in a company.
  - Provides guidance on strategies to secure needed capital while preserving founder autonomy and vision.

- **VC and entrepreneur dual viewpoint**
  - Based on Bussgang’s experience on both sides of the table: as a venture capitalist and as an entrepreneur raising money.
  - Offers insight into how each side views deals, risk, and company growth.

- **Negotiating with investors**
  - Practical advice on negotiating with venture capitalists.
  - Focus on achieving the best possible terms for the business, including control, governance, and economic outcomes.

- **Term sheet and deal dynamics (implied by topic)**
  - Helps readers understand the structure and implications of typical VC deal terms.
  - Connects term-sheet details to long-term founder control and company trajectory.

- **Interviews with leading entrepreneurs and VCs**
  - Includes interviews with successful entrepreneurs and prominent venture capitalists.
  - Provides real-world stories and perspectives that illustrate how deals are made and relationships are managed.

- **From startup to IPO focus**
  - Frames venture capital as a long-term partnership that can extend from early-stage funding through IPO.
  - Highlights how founder–investor relationships evolve over the company’s life cycle.

## Use Cases
- Founders preparing to raise their first venture round.
- Entrepreneurs seeking to better understand VC incentives and mindset.
- Startup teams looking to negotiate term sheets and governance structures.
- Readers wanting case-based insights from experienced VCs and founders.

# I Will Teach You to Be Rich (Second Edition)

Ramit Sethi’s practical personal finance book offering a 6‑week program for automating money, managing debt, and building long‑term wealth. It’s not a startup operations book but is very useful for founders who need personal financial stability and systems while taking risks building a company.

## Overview

The Creative Curve reveals that creativity is not magical talent but a skill anyone can develop through deliberate practice.

## The Creative Curve

The sweet spot between familiarity and novelty - ideas that feel fresh yet accessible.

## Four Laws of Creativity

1. **Consumption**: Immerse yourself in your field
2. **Imitation**: Copy and remix existing work
3. **Creative Communities**: Find your support network
4. **Iterations**: Repeat, refine, improve

## Pattern Recognition

Creativity comes from recognizing patterns others miss, enabled by extensive consumption and iteration.

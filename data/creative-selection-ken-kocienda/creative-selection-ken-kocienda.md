## Overview

Creative Selection is a rare insider's look at how Apple creates products, told by a software engineer who worked on the original iPhone keyboard and iPad.

## The Seven Essential Elements

1. **Inspiration**: Thinking big ideas
2. **Collaboration**: Working together
3. **Craft**: Applying skill
4. **Diligence**: Doing the necessary hard work
5. **Decisiveness**: Making tough choices
6. **Taste**: Developing a refined sense of judgment
7. **Empathy**: Trying to see from other perspectives

## The Demo-Driven Culture

At Apple, demos were the primary means of:

- Communicating ideas
- Getting feedback
- Making decisions
- Showing progress

## Key Lessons

- Make demos, not presentations
- Iterate relentlessly on details
- Trust your taste
- Sweat the details
- Ship products you're proud of

## Impact on Product Development

The book reveals how Apple's unique culture of demos, taste, and iteration produced revolutionary products like the iPhone.

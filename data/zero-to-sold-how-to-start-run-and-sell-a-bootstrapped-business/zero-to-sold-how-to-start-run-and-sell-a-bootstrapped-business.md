## Overview

Zero to Sold is Arvid Kahl's detailed account of building, growing, and selling FeedbackPanda, a SaaS business he co-founded and sold for a life-changing sum after just two years.

## Key Sections

### 1. Finding a Problem Worth Solving
- Identifying validated problems
- Understanding your audience
- Finding product-market fit

### 2. Building the Product
- MVP development
- Technical decisions for bootstrappers
- Building for sustainability

### 3. Growing the Business
- Customer acquisition strategies
- Retention and churn reduction
- Building sustainable revenue

### 4. Preparing for Sale
- Making your business sellable
- Finding buyers
- Negotiating the deal

## Bootstrapper's Philosophy

The book emphasizes building profitable, sustainable businesses without external funding, focusing on serving customers well and creating real value.

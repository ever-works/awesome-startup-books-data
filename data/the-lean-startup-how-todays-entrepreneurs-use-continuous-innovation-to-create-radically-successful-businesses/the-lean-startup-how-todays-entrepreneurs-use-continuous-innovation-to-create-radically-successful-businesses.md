# The Lean Startup: How Today’s Entrepreneurs Use Continuous Innovation to Create Radically Successful Businesses

The Lean Startup by Eric Ries introduces the build–measure–learn feedback loop, minimum viable products, and validated learning, providing a systematic methodology for founders to reduce risk, iterate quickly, and build successful startups under extreme uncertainty.

## Overview

Tribes: We Need You to Lead Us by Seth Godin explores how anyone can build a tribe—a group of people connected to each other, a leader, and an idea.

## Core Concept

A tribe is any group of people, large or small, who are connected to one another, a leader, and an idea. The internet has eliminated barriers to leadership, allowing anyone to gather a tribe.

## Three Steps to Building a Tribe

1. **The Desire to Change Things**: A shared vision for the future
2. **The Ability to Connect**: Building relationships and communication
3. **The Willingness to Lead**: Taking responsibility for the tribe

## What Tribes Need

- A shared interest
- A way to communicate
- A leader to champion the cause
- A movement that matters

## The New Leadership

- You don't need authority to lead
- Leadership is about creating change
- Challenge the status quo
- Connect people to each other
- Tell a story worth spreading

## Applications for Founders

- Build communities around your product
- Create movements, not just companies
- Lead through connection and purpose
- Empower customers to become evangelists
- Use the internet to gather your tribe

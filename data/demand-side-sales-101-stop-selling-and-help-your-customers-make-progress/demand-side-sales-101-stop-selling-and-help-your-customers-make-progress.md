## Overview

Bob Moesta (co-creator of JTBD theory with Clay Christensen) shows how to sell by understanding the customer's buying journey, not your sales process.

## Demand-Side vs. Supply-Side

**Supply-Side** (traditional):
- Push your product
- Talk about features
- Overcome objections
- Close the sale

**Demand-Side**:
- Understand their struggle
- Help them make progress
- Reduce anxieties
- Enable their decision

## The Timeline of Buying

### First Thought
What triggered them to start looking?

### Passive Looking
Casual exploration, no urgency

### Active Looking
Seeking specific solutions

### Deciding
Narrowing options, building consensus

### Onboarding
First use, achieving initial success

## Forces of Progress

Two pushing, two pulling:

**Push**:
- Push of the situation (pain)
- Pull of the new solution (attraction)

**Pull (resistance)**:
- Anxiety of new solution (fear)
- Habit of present (inertia)

## For B2B Sales

- Understand their struggle
- Map their timeline
- Reduce anxieties
- Address habits/inertia
- Help build consensus
